and what new ones we should add. This can make it easier to
submit payments and may help reduce or eliminate fees you pay to
your card issuer for foreign currency transactions

EUR to

in EUR on your account, it'll be
converted to an equivalent amount of
SEK when the currency change
occurs. The exchange rate to be used will be from
