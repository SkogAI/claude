<p>If you use AI seriously to think or work, a surprising share of your documents are probably already written by agents.</p>
<p>Most of us at Every are using <u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiSW50cm9kdWNpbmcgUHJvb2YiLCJwb3N0X2lkIjozOTY4LCJwb3N0X3R5cGUiOiJwb3N0IiwidXJsIjoiaHR0cHM6Ly9ldmVyeS50by92aWJlLWNoZWNrL2dwdC01LTMtY29kZXgiLCJwb3NpdGlvbiI6N30=" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Codex</a></u> to generate plan documents, <u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiSW50cm9kdWNpbmcgUHJvb2YiLCJwb3N0X2lkIjozOTY4LCJwb3N0X3R5cGUiOiJwb3N0IiwidXJsIjoiaHR0cHM6Ly9ldmVyeS50by92aWJlLWNoZWNrL3ZpYmUtY2hlY2stY2xhdWRlLWNvd29yay1pcy1jbGF1ZGUtY29kZS1mb3ItdGhlLXJlc3Qtb2YtdXMiLCJwb3NpdGlvbiI6OH0=" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Claude Cowork</a></u> to write research reports, and <u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiSW50cm9kdWNpbmcgUHJvb2YiLCJwb3N0X2lkIjozOTY4LCJwb3N0X3R5cGUiOiJwb3N0IiwidXJsIjoiaHR0cHM6Ly9ldmVyeS50by9ndWlkZXMvY2xhdy1zY2hvb2wiLCJwb3NpdGlvbiI6OX0=" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">OpenClaws</a></u> to create strategy memos. <h2><strong>What is Proof?</strong></h2>
<p>Most word processors still assume a human is doing the writing and AI is helping at the margins for brainstorming, making rewrite suggestions, or producing a first draft. Proof flips that around. It’s a document editor built for the kinds of documents agents are increasingly writing: bug reports, product requirement documents, implementation plans, research briefs, copy audits, strategy documents, memos, and proposals.</p>
<p>It supports live edits with multiple collaborators, allows you to see and leave comments, and lets you track changes. </p>
<p>But it also has what you would expect of a document editor built for agents. Proof:</p>
<ol>
<li data-list="bullet">
<span class="ql-ui" contenteditable="false"></span><strong>Is agent-native:</strong> Anything you can do in Proof, your agent can do just as easily.</li>
<li data-list="bullet">
<span class="ql-ui" contenteditable="false"></span><strong>Tracks provenance:</strong> A colored rail on the left side of every document tracks who wrote what. <strong>Green</strong> means human, <strong>purple</strong> means AI.</li>
<li data-list="bullet">
<span class="ql-ui" contenteditable="false"></span><strong>Is login-free and open-source:</strong> We want Proof to be your agent’s favorite document editor.</li>
</ol>
