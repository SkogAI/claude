Mobile apps in Replit, new voice AI models, private apps for free users, & more



 <https://replit.com>



Friday, February 6, 2026


The year is flying by, and so are product updates! January kicked off with our 
team returning from the holidays recharged and ready to ship.

Here's what we released last month:


 * Build mobile apps directly in Replit, with a simplified App Store 
submission flow
 * New voice AI models available without any API keys in AI Integrations
 * MORE new models in AI integrations - GPT 5.2, Opus 4.5, & Gemini 3
 * Private apps for free accounts

and more! Let's dive in.




📱Mobile apps in Replit



 This is the one we've been building toward. You can now build iOS & Android 
appsfrom a simple prompt.

Agent can create mobile apps 
<https://docs.replit.com/replitai/building-mobile-apps>, with every feature 
Replit has to offer: databases, Stripe payments, and OpenAI Integrations if you 
need them.

 Scan a QR code to preview instantly on your phone with Expo Go, then publish 
to the App Store with one click.


New models in AI integrations



 The latest and greatest AI models are now available in Integrations 
<https://docs.replit.com/replitai/replit-ai-integrations>: GPT-5.2, Claude Opus 
4.5, Gemini 3 Flash Preview, Gemini 3 Pro Preview, Gemini 3 Pro Image Preview.

 We've also added audio support for GPT-4o Transcribe and GPT-4o Transcribe 
Mini for speech-to-text & GPT-4o Audio and GPT-4o Audio Mini for generating 
speech.

Swap models in your apps with a few clicks. No API key management required. 
Opus 4.6 & GPT-5.3 coming soon!


📌 Other new features


 * Automation preview graphs – See your automation steps in seconds instead of 
waiting minutes
 * `replit ai` Shell command – Chat with LLMs directly in your Shell
 * Redesigned tool calls UI – Cleaner Agent chat with collapsible tool calls
 * Web search in Automations 
<https://docs.replit.com/replitai/agents-and-automations> – Build scheduled 
research tools and news summaries without API keys
 * Open in Replit with prefilled prompts – Create shareable links 
<https://docs.replit.com/replitai/open-in-replit> that open Replit with your

 * LinkedIn skills verification – LinkedIn launched AI-powered coding 
challenges built on Replit

 You can find weekly updates and more in our changelog
