We adopted new technologies to mature our stack:
 * Redis: to cache Prompts and queue events

 * Clickhouse: Open Source OLAP database to store all our tracing data and optimize analytical reads

 * S3: used to store multi-modal data and raw tracing events

 * Asynchronous workers: dedicated container to execute asynchronous tasks
