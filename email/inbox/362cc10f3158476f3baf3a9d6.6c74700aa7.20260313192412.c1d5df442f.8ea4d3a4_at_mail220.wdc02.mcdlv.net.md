** Multi-Agent Persistence
------------------------------------------------------------

Many of you are hearing about Dolt for the first time via Beads (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=115020eae8&e=6c74700aa7) , Gas Town (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=f186d284c4&e=6c74700aa7) , or Wasteland (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=cc33c351cf&e=6c74700aa7) . How did Dolt end up as the persistence layer for Beads and thus powering Gas Town and Wasteland?

My latest article (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=45d4566116&e=6c74700aa7) explains how Dolt is perfect for multi-agent persistence. It turns out multi-agent systems require version-controlled structured data accessed concurrently. Dolt is the only database that does that.
