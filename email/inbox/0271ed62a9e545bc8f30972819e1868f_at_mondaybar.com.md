Our plan was one big cruise every year. But then we got the opportunity to organise a cruise on M/S Birka Gotland, one of the finest, but not biggest cruise liners on the Baltic Sea. She was decommissioned in 2020 at the beginning of covid, and was held in port at Mariehamn until Gotlandsbolaget and Viking Line decided to take her over as a joint venture last year. And after two successful cruises with Viking Cinderella we have been welcomed back. M/S Birka Gotland is a pure cruise ship with no car deck, and her regular route is Stockholm-Mariehamn, but can also go to other destinations, such as Visby, Gotland, especially during summer. But for us, we will this time make a shorter 21hr trip to Mariehamn, Åland. Many of our guests have requested that we also do shorter, one-day cruises, like parents who can have a hard time to be away longer.
