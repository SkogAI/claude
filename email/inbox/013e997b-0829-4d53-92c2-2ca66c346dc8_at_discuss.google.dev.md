    This is promising but hyped architecture - the core idea is solid, but the practical implications haven’t been stress-tested enough.

∙	Genuinely novel approach to the context length problem. Instead of just extending windows (like Gemini’s 2M tokens), it gives agents algorithmic control over context. That’s a fundamentally different paradigm. 
∙	Task decomposition via recursion makes intuitive sense - it mirrors how humans solve complex problems by breaking them down. 
∙	The lazy file loading extension is the most practical contribution - enterprises won’t benefit from RLM unless it can work with real data sources, not just in-memory strings.
