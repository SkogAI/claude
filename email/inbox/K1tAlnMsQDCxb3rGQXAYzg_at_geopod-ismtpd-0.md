But if you are short on time, here's the gist:

🔐 *End-to-end encryption.* All your vaults are always end-to-end encrypted using your encryption password.

🔑 *Your encryption password stays only with you.* It is never stored alongside your data or sent over the network. This makes it impossible for someone to learn anything by intercepting your data while it's in transit.

⚠️ *Lost encryption password, lost access.* Without passwords, vaults cannot be restored by anyone, even us. So, save it in a safe place, like a password manager.

👥 *You control who can access your vaults.* When sharing a vault with a teammate, you decide who can access your data and whether or not to share credentials.
