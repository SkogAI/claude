<h5><strong>Spiral is experimenting with agent-to-agent workflows</strong></h5>
<p>Two days after the release of Anthropic’s new <u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiVGhlIE1pc3NpbmcgTGF5ZXIgaW4gQUkgQWRvcHRpb24iLCJwb3N0X2lkIjo0MTA3LCJwb3N0X3R5cGUiOiJwb3N0IiwidXJsIjoiaHR0cHM6Ly9wbGF0Zm9ybS5jbGF1ZGUuY29tL2RvY3MvZW4vbWFuYWdlZC1hZ2VudHMvb3ZlcnZpZXciLCJwb3NpdGlvbiI6NDJ9" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">managed agents</a></u>, <strong>Marcus Moretti,</strong> general manager of <strong><u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiVGhlIE1pc3NpbmcgTGF5ZXIgaW4gQUkgQWRvcHRpb24iLCJwb3N0X2lkIjo0MTA3LCJwb3N0X3R5cGUiOiJwb3N0IiwidXJsIjoiaHR0cDovL3dyaXRld2l0aHNwaXJhbC5jb20iLCJwb3NpdGlvbiI6NDN9" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Spiral</a></u></strong>, has set them up to power Spiral’s API. The setup lets an external agent (rather than a human) hand off a writing task to Spiral, where the two agents interview each other behind the scenes before producing a draft with no human input required. Marcus built a new API endpoint for this flow and added an API label in Spiral’s UI so users can distinguish between agent-generated and human-initiated conversations. The API also now supports attachments and smarter default selections for workspace and style. Conversations via API show up in your Spiral chat history with an “API” label, so you can pick up where the agent left off.</p>

<h2>Alignment </h2>
<p><strong>The wrong fight. </strong>I don’t know what’s in the water in Utah, but whatever it is, I want more of it, because the state is leading the country on using AI in healthcare. </p>
<p>Legion Health, a Y Combinator-backed San Francisco startup, has been cleared to use AI in Utah to renew a handful of psychiatric prescriptions, including Prozac and Zoloft, for patients who are already stable and on an established treatment plan. It’s the second AI healthcare pilot approved there, and it’s replacing the barrages of emails from patients who are stable on the same dose, contacting their clinicians who are already buried in administrative work, who have to produce a piece of paper that says yes, same drug, same dose, carry on. This is often done outside of working hours, and without any reimbursement. </p>
<p>To ensure the <u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiVGhlIE1pc3NpbmcgTGF5ZXIgaW4gQUkgQWRvcHRpb24iLCJwb3N0X2lkIjo0MTA3LCJwb3N0X3R5cGUiOiJwb3N0IiwidXJsIjoiaHR0cHM6Ly93d3cucHltbnRzLmNvbS9hcnRpZmljaWFsLWludGVsbGlnZW5jZS0yLzIwMjYvbGVnaW9uLWhlYWx0aC1haS1jbGVhcmVkLXRvLXByb3ZpZGUtZmFzdGVyLXJlZmlsbHMtZm9yLXV0YWgtcGF0aWVudHMvIiwicG9zaXRpb24iOjQ0fQ==" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">pilot is safe</a></u>, the first 250 AI renewals are reviewed by a physician before anything reaches a pharmacy, and the AI has to agree with that physician more than 98 percent of the time before it can proceed independently. The next 1,000 renewals are then reviewed, with an even higher threshold of 99 percent before the oversight shifts to randomized monthly testing, with Legion filing monthly reports on accuracy and any adverse outcomes throughout.</p>
<p>Yet both the <u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiVGhlIE1pc3NpbmcgTGF5ZXIgaW4gQUkgQWRvcHRpb24iLCJwb3N0X2lkIjo0MTA3LCJwb3N0X3R5cGUiOiJwb3N0IiwidXJsIjoiaHR0cHM6Ly9mdXR1cmlzbS5jb20vaGVhbHRoLW1lZGljaW5lL3N0YXJ0dXAtYWktc3lzdGVtLXByZXNjcmliZS1wc3ljaGlhdHJpYy1tZWRpY2F0aW9uIiwicG9zaXRpb24iOjQ1fQ==" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">tech coverage</a></u> and members of the <u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiVGhlIE1pc3NpbmcgTGF5ZXIgaW4gQUkgQWRvcHRpb24iLCJwb3N0X2lkIjo0MTA3LCJwb3N0X3R5cGUiOiJwb3N0IiwidXJsIjoiaHR0cHM6Ly93d3cudGhldmVyZ2UuY29tL2FpLWFydGlmaWNpYWwtaW50ZWxsaWdlbmNlLzkwNjUyNS9haS1jaGF0Ym90LXByZXNjcmliZS1yZWZpbGwtcHN5Y2hpYXRyaWMtZHJ1Z3MiLCJwb3NpdGlvbiI6NDZ9" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">medical establishment</a></u> have deemed it too risky. The criticism splits into two camps: prescribing error, and the app’s insufficiency to improve access to the patients who need care most. On prescribing error, the hard clinical judgment has already been made by a human; what the AI is doing is confirming that nothing has changed, which it has to get right 98 percent of the time before it’s allowed to proceed unsupervised. </p>
<p>On access, it’s true that you have to already be in treatment to use this service, but if a psychiatrist in rural Utah who typically spends part of their day processing renewal emails for stable patients no longer needs to do so, they have more time for the patients who need them.</p>
<p>Most of Utah’s counties are designated mental health provider shortage areas, leaving around <u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiVGhlIE1pc3NpbmcgTGF5ZXIgaW4gQUkgQWRvcHRpb24iLCJwb3N0X2lkIjo0MTA3LCJwb3N0X3R5cGUiOiJwb3N0IiwidXJsIjoiaHR0cHM6Ly9jb21tZXJjZS51dGFoLmdvdi9haS9hZ3JlZW1lbnRzL2FpLWxlZ2lvbi1oZWFsdGgvIiwicG9zaXRpb24iOjQ3fQ==" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">500,000 residents</a></u> without adequate psychiatric care.</p>
<p>Physician risk-aversion is one of medicine’s great virtues in the right context, but renewing a stable prescription is not that context, and dressing up administrative inertia as a patient safety concern doesn’t make it one.—<em><u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiVGhlIE1pc3NpbmcgTGF5ZXIgaW4gQUkgQWRvcHRpb24iLCJwb3N0X2lkIjo0MTA3LCJwb3N0X3R5cGUiOiJwb3N0IiwidXJsIjoiaHR0cHM6Ly94LmNvbS9Bc2h3aW5yZWFkcyIsInBvc2l0aW9uIjo0OH0=" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Ashwin Sharma</a></u></em></p>
