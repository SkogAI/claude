    <div role="separator" style="margin-top: 24px; margin-bottom: 24px; height: 0.5px; background-color: #e7e5e4; line-height: 1px">&zwj;</div>		<p>Hi,</p>
<p>Did Capacities feel like too much when you first tried it? Maybe you weren’t sure where to start, or it didn’t quite click. You’re not alone, and we’ve been working on something for you.</p>
<p>We’ve just launched a brand new onboarding built around the feedback we were getting from users: people wanted to get into the app faster, understand the basics without drowning in tutorials, and actually feel that Capacities is simple and intuitive rather than overwhelming.</p>
<p>So we rebuilt it with a few clear goals:</p>
<ol>
<li><strong>Flatten the learning curve</strong> so it feels less steep from day one.</li>
<li><strong>Get you into the app quickly</strong> with just enough guidance, no information overload.</li>
<li><strong>Show you the core concepts in practice</strong> so you can see you don’t need to read everything or watch endless tutorials to get started.</li>
<li><strong>Make it fun and engaging</strong> so starting fresh feels exciting, not daunting.</li>
</ol>
<p>Whether you’re new or tried Capacities before and it didn’t stick, we’d love you to give the new onboarding a go. You can run through it again anytime in Settings, or you’ll be prompted when you create a new space.</p>
<p>If you’re curious about how we approached it, you can read our Founder’s take <a href="https://capacities.io/blog/new-onboarding" style="font-weight: 500; color: #1c1917; text-decoration: underline">here</a>.</p>
<p>We’d love to hear what you think.</p>
