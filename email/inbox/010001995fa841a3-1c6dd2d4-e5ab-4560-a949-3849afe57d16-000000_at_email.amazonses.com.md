Hey! As is tradition at this point, my weekly email is not on time 😭

Just over a month ago I talked about [not knowing what to work on](https://x.com/iannuttall/status/1955329993921245464) and how I was looking for that one product people would pay for.

I acquired [ilo.so](http://ilo.so) as part of that goal, with plans to turn it into a toolkit for content creators.

This all got me thinking that maybe I don't need a product? 

Maybe I could just continue to do what I love, building proof of concepts, micro-apps, and using the latest AI models while sharing what I do?

If I could do that and be sponsored by companies like OpenAI, Anthropic, Google etc (just like open source maintainers get sponsored) I could get paid to just do what I love.

The idea is that I want to help as many people as possible and give everything away for free - while making a modest income to be able to do that!

I don't need $50k+ MRR to live the life I want.

($10k will do just fine, tyvm 😂)

Is anybody out there able to fund themselves fully from sponsorships? Would love to see some examples of content creators who have been able to do this.

Or anybody working at a big AI co that wants to sponsor me??

Anyway...

Personally, Opus 4.1 is still my go-to model for planning and coding and it's also a lot quicker than GPT-5 in general - but OAI are catching up very fast!
