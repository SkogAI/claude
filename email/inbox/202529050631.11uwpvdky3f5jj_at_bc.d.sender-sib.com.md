**Master Deep Research Workflow**
=================================

Complex questions demand smarter search. Our new Deep Research guide shows you how to build workflows that think like expert researchers – identifying knowledge gaps, running targeted searches, and delivering comprehensive reports. The key lies in powerful components:

 

[Loop Variables](https://b6usd.r.a.d.sendibm1.com/mk/cl/f/sh/7nVU1aA2nfwFRhYhzxEnS3NaN2SuWPe/90nLo34AOUXe): Unlike normal variables that flow linearly (Node 1 → Node 2 → Node 3), loop variables create a knowledge network. They store and recall outputs from every past cycle, ensuring each iteration builds on previous discoveries without repeating work.

 

[Think Tool Plugin](https://b6usd.r.a.d.sendibm1.com/mk/cl/f/sh/7nVU1aA2nfy8RE40yWTFUPhOXOxhGMA/895yhd79CDwq): This new plugin adds a reasoning sandbox to any LLM, letting it pause, verify rules, and plan before acting. It's like giving your AI a moment to think, boosting accuracy on multi-tool uses and policy-driven tasks.

 

Read the full [guide](https://b6usd.r.a.d.sendibm1.com/mk/cl/f/sh/7nVU1aA2ng01QkZJx5hhWm1ChlSU0Ig/Wp8B2fZoaY2y) and [try it now.](https://b6usd.r.a.d.sendibm1.com/mk/cl/f/sh/7nVU1aA2ng1uQH4cvew9Z8L0s7xGkFC/Js10xanviRjv)
