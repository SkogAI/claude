Live Session: Track Objects Through Occlusion
*********************************************

Building reliable object tracking into your computer vision pipeline is essential for counting items, analyzing movement patterns, and monitoring video streams over time. However, maintaining consistent tracks when objects intersect or temporarily disappear behind obstacles can easily break traditional logic.

In this week’s live session ( https://luma.com/pd4hodix?utm_campaign=Webinar%3A+April+23+object+tracking&utm_content=webinar+tracking&utm_medium=email_action&utm_source=email ), Roboflow Machine Learning Engineer Lee Clement will demonstrate how to solve these challenges using the newly integrated tracking blocks in Roboflow Workflows:

*
Implement State-of-the-Art Trackers: See how to drag and drop SORT and OC-SORT algorithms directly into your pipelines.
* Handle Heavy Occlusions: Learn when to deploy OC-SORT to maintain consistent tracks during non-linear motion or when objects are temporarily obstructed.
*
Optimize for Real-Time Speed: Discover how to configure SORT for high-speed, real-time pipelines where low latency is critical.

Join us to explore how to build a custom tracking workflow from scratch and deploy it seamlessly across different environments.
