📱 Logs are Coming to Mobile
---------------------------

Last month, we introduced session logs on the desktop. Now, they are rolling out to mobile.

Logs are already being recorded on Android and will soon be released on iOS. This means your terminal sessions are now saved and synced, even when you are troubleshooting on the go. So, when you return to the desktop, you can review the log from your mobile session and continue where you left off.

For teams, it works even better. It allows you to see the last session regardless of who has connected. Logs add transparency and help teams quickly pinpoint what change caused the issue.

If your team requires more than the last session log per host, request a limit increase here ( https://termius.typeform.com/to/E9XVyoIb#email=emil.skogsund@gmail.com ).

-------------------------------------
⚙️ Run Commands Across Multiple Hosts
-------------------------------------

Whether you are applying a security patch or updating packages across your fleet of hosts, it can take hours of work. In Termius, you can do it within minutes with these three steps:
* Create a Snippet — a command or shell script that you need to run.
* Double-click on a Snippet and select your target hosts.
* Click "Run."
You will see a new tab showing each connection and the execution status. If anything fails, you can jump into the session directly and fix it. The new Snippet tab supports split view and focus mode, giving you a clear view of each host's status.
