Hi there!

We want to inform you about an important change that may affect your AI agent workflows. Anthropic has announced the deprecation of the Claude 3 Sonnet model effective July 21, 2025 at 9 AM PT.

Claude 3 Sonnet will be marked deprecated in our list of LLM models and will be removed after the deprecation date. To ensure uninterrupted performance, we recommend updating your AI agent workflows to use newer Claude models, such as Claude 3.5 Sonnet, which offers improved capabilities.

Please review your existing workflows and update any that rely on Claude 3 Sonnet as soon as possible.
