Discover our new parsing modes for better accuracy!

Hi LlamaCloud user,

We’ve updated our LlamaCloud parsing modes to make things simpler — and smarter — for you. These changes are based on our observations across thousands of users as well as enterprise customers and the common issues they ran into.

What’s Changed:

- The old Fast 1-credit mode is now in Advanced Settings→ Parse without AI — we found it didn't meet most users' needs but it's still available for those who need it.
- The new default is 3-credit “Cost-effective” mode — same price as the old balanced mode, but significantly improved accuracy.
- 10-credit “Agentic” is ideal for high-quality results and works well for most docs.
- New! 90-credit “Agentic Plus” mode for the most difficult parsing tasks — tables split across pages, handwriting, image overlays, etc.
