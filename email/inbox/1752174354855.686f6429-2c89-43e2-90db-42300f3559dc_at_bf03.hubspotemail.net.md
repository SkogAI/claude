Creating Your First Zen Agent

- Click the "..." menu in the top right of your chat window, select "Agents," then "Add Custom Agent"
- Configure your agent by entering:

- A descriptive name
- Sharing preferences (personal or organization-wide)
- Command keyword (how you'll activate it)
- Custom instructions for its behavior

- Enhance your agent with tools:

- Web search capabilities
- Integrations with your development tools
- MCP servers for specialized functions

- Use your agent anytime by typing "/command_name" in the chat window
