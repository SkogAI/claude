🎻 MAESTRO SYMPHONY

Symphony is a new way to contribute to open source projects. Instead of writing
code yourself, you can donate CPU time and tokens leveraging AI agents work on
issues and enhancements. Pick an issue from the Symphony board that interests
you, run it locally, and watch your contribution come to life.

This idea emerged from discussions on our Discord, and we think it's pretty
novel. We're democratizing open source contributions, enabling people who may
not be traditional engineers to make meaningful contributions to projects they
care about.

How It Works

1. Browse Symphony projects you'd like to support
2. Pick an issue and launch it as a Maestro agent
3. Donate CPU and tokens to power the contribution
4. A PR is submitted automatically on completion

We're starting with Maestro itself as the first Symphony project. If you know of
other open source projects that could benefit from this model, please reach out
to them and have them register. We'd love to see a ton of different projects
here.

Explore Symphony https://docs.runmaestro.ai/symphony
