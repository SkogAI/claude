Hi,
 
Earlier today (June 18, 2025, 05:30am PST), Composio experienced a service disruption of approximately 45 minutes.
 
We are writing to let you know about what happened and the mitigation steps taken.


What Happened

 * During a routine Cloudflare billing renewal, one of Cloudflare’s critical services failed.

 * This failure disabled our Cloudflare Workers, which power request routing and authentication.

 * As a result, requests to composio.dev returned Rate Limit Errors and the app became unavailable.

 * Although Cloudflare marked the incident “resolved,” residual errors persisted (see their Incident Report
   [https://www.cloudflarestatus.com/incidents/tkfy82b8sc3y]) and continued to disrupt our traffic routing until we intervened.


What We’ve Done

 * Full Restore: Worked closely with Cloudflare support to clear stale configurations and fully restore all Workers.


Preventive Measures

 * Redundant Routing: Building a secondary routing path that automatically takes over if our primary Workers fail.

 * Enhanced SLAs: Negotiating stricter renewal-window guarantees and advanced notifications with Cloudflare.

 
We know even a brief outage can be disruptive, and we appreciate your patience. If you have any questions or concerns, please
reply to this email or email me personally at soham@composio.dev.
