Dear Monday Bar family, It’s been a while, but now we’re back again with a local event in Stockholm before the May cruise. On Saturday May 15 at Slakthuset, 12 djs will spin trance, techno, psy, hardstyle and hardcore. The lineup for Monday Bar: The Grand 2025 Cruise is slowly shaping up. We’re putting up the djs that we book on our homepage continuously, but there are still a few left. All will be presented individually on our social media. Our dress theme this time are these inspirational words: FUNFAIR, CIRCUS, MERRY-GO-ROUND, TIVOLI. The cruise has basically been sold out since October, but occasionally a few cabins are released again, this time from the ship’s entertainment department. When we last checked there were 5 available standard cabins in different classes. They can be booked för 3-4 persons (but not for only 1-2). Also, those of you who have booked a cabin for only 1-3 persons can make room for extra guests, up to 4 persons in standard cabins. To upgrade, you can get in touch with the ferry line booking office by email or phone. They also have a chat on the website.
