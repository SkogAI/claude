Building things for yourself worked out pretty well for me this past week.

My first email newsletter sent from my own tool had a 39.3% open rate.

Considering I haven't sent a regular email in a pretty long time, that's not bad at all!

The entire app was built in a [new simple tech stack](https://x.com/iannuttall/status/1942899136732221889) I have been using. Claude Code seems to do very well with it - even without a lot of rules.

I've been sick the last couple of weeks so haven't had much chance to record any new videos on Claude Code for [**my YouTube channel**](https://www.youtube.com/@inuttall), but I did just hit the 4,000 hours of watch time needed to monetise with ads. 🎉

Hopefully I feel well enough soon to hit record. If you have any topic ideas just reply and let me know!

I've also been working on my own tool that will check what content others are creating and, importantly, what comments they get, to figure out interesting angle and topics to cover.

[!NOTICE]
The idea of building personal productivity agents is very interesting to me. A lot of the content for this newsletter is sourced from my X posts but I want to go a step (or two) further:

- Track popular posts from people I follow + trust
- Look at replies and find questions to answer
- Build a pipeline of data into Gemini 2.5 Flash (or another cheap model with huge context window)
- Create summaries and ideas
- Pass to a smarter agent like Claude 4, GPT 4.1, or maybe Kimi K2, who I am hearing is pretty good at writing
- Get drafts for email newsletter and YouTube videos every Monday

TLDR; I'm very lazy with planning and if I can build agents to do the grunt work here I can focus on the stuff I enjoy like building stuff and talking about it!
[/!NOTICE]
