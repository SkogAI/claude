It works by requiring at least two pieces of evidence to prove that you are you. 

 Your password is the first piece of evidence. The second could be any of these:


	- Your fingerprint scanned on your device
	- A limited-time verification code sent to your email
	- A unique code sent via SMS or text message


So even if someone manages to steal your password, they can’t access your account without the second piece of the puzzle.
