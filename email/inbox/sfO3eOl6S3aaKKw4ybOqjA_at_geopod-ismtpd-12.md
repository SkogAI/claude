IDE-style autocomplete
----------------------

Boost your productivity with IDE-style autocomplete in your terminal. Autocomplete allows you to seamlessly complete paths, command history, snippets, and passwords. It can even turn plain text into commands with AI—all to make your work in the terminal easier.

We believe that autocomplete should be both easy and powerful. We aim to make the terminal experience playful, allowing you to run even complex commands with just the arrow key and Enter. In the recent release, we simplified interactions with autocomplete:

* *Enter to paste password:* When the terminal prompts for a password, like with sudo, the password you saved in a host is now preselected. Simply press Enter to paste.
* *Instant AI suggestion:* type your prompt in the terminal, press down for AI suggestions, edit with Tab, or execute with Enter.

Enable autocomplete in Settings -> Terminal -> Autocomplete. Join our Discord ( https://discord.gg/KpZw78pRcF ) to share your feedback and suggest improvements.

FIDO2 Keys Are Now Free in Termius
----------------------------------

FIDO2 security keys in Termius are now free for everyone on all platforms. That means even with a Starter plan, you can generate FIDO2 keys in Termius, export public keys to your servers, and connect securely with just one click—on mobile or desktop.

Hardware keys are one of the industry's most secure ways to authenticate via SSH. Unlike regular SSH keys, which are stored on a hard drive and can be stolen, FIDO2 keys never expose the private key, require physical presence to connect, and make it impossible for attackers to extract credentials.

Enhanced security often comes with added complexity and setup time, which is not the case with Termius. It securely synchronizes your vaults across devices, including Hosts and information about FIDO2 keys. It guarantees a seamless and secure one-click connection from mobile and desktop. Open the app, click on a host, plug in your key, and connect.
