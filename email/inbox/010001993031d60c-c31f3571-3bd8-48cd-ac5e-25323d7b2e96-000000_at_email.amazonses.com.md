We’re thrilled to launch Modal Notebooks: a new, powerful cloud-hosted notebook built for ML experimentation and collaboration.
 



 
Here's what makes Notebooks different:
 
⚡ Sub-5s cold boots on custom images. No more waiting for large ML images to load.
💥 Instant GPU access up to 8× H100/B200 GPUs.
