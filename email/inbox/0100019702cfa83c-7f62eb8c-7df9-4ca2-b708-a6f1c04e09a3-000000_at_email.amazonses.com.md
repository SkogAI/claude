📐 Langfuse Evaluator Library

Evaluating your application is a crucial step in turning a promising prototype into a production-ready system and for continuously
monitoring its performance. We want to make this as easy and powerful as possible, which is why we’re launching the Langfuse
Evaluator Library.
The Evaluator Library lets you use LLM-as-a-Judge evals to monitor and score key metrics for your LLM applications or AI agents.
To kick things off, we’ve integrated RAGAS evaluators [https://docs.ragas.io/en/stable/] to assess metrics such as context
relevance, goal accuracy, and SQL semantic equivalence. You can also use Langfuse-managed evaluators to test aspects like
conciseness and hallucination.
Why you’ll love it:
 * Always up to date: Langfuse maintains and regularly upgrades each evaluator, so you benefit from the latest research by
   default.

 * Broad metric coverage: Context precision & recall, hallucination, answer correctness, faithfulness, SQL semantic equivalence,
   topic adherence/refusal, toxicity, and more.

 * Custom evaluators: Set up and run your own evaluators tailored to your specific use case.

We’ve also greatly improved the evaluator UX, making setup and configuration within Langfuse simpler and more intuitive. We’d love
your feedback!
→ Check out the full changelog [https://langfuse.com/changelog/2025-05-24-langfuse-evaluator-library]
