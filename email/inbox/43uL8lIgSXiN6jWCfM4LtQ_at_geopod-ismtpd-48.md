Workspaces: Take Control of Your Focus
--------------------------------------

The year has just started, and you already have a pile of work. Staying focused is hard when trying to action all these tasks across multiple connections at once. A few long-running commands here, a log tail there, and suddenly your attention is divided.

Workspaces help to manage your attention without losing awareness of what's going on.

First, create a Workspace from a New Tab by selecting several recent connections, and the Workspace will be opened in Focus mode.

Focus mode, when deep focus matters
-----------------------------------

Focus mode lets you concentrate on a single task without being distracted by your environment.

One terminal takes the screen, while others stay visible in a side list. When a long-running task finishes, you'll see a notification on its tab, so you know exactly when it's time to switch context.

When autocomplete is enabled, Workspaces show the current working directory and the command running in each tab. You can see what's happening at a glance without switching tabs.

You stay focused, without going blind.

Broadcast to all terminals
--------------------------

Broadcast works in both Split view and Focus mode. Send the same command to all terminals in a Workspace when you need to apply changes or run checks across hosts.

Restore your work the next day
------------------------------

Save a Workspace as a template by clicking the dot in the tab to restore it later. No rebuilding your environment, no reopening the same connections by hand.

Templates can even remember the working directory and the commands that were running.

The next time you open your terminal, let Workspaces handle the chaos.
