🔖 *Introducing Bookmarks for Session Logs*
------------------------------------------

Troubleshooting often leaves you with discoveries, fixes, and decisions that you or your teammates may need later. Until now, this information lived only in the most recent log, making it easy to lose track of important work.

Bookmarks change that
---------------------

You can now bookmark any session log in your vault and add notes that explain it: what you were fixing, what you found, and what should happen next. Bookmarked sessions sync across all your devices, so you always have full context, no matter where you connect from.

For hosts stored in team vaults, bookmarked sessions are shared automatically, giving your team clear, reliable context for:

* *Better incident reviews:* all decisions and observations are recorded, not recreated from memory.
* *Faster root-cause analysis:* the trail of changes is complete and easy to follow.
* *Cleaner handoffs:* knowledge stays in your vault, not scattered across devices or teammates.

👉 Read the full blog post ( https://termius.com/blog/remember-what-was-done-with-bookmarks-for-session-logs?utm_source=newsletter ) to see how Bookmarks work and what they unlock next.
