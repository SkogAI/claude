We hope this message finds you well. We are writing to inform you about an important security update for your Stars Account account.

Effective September 8th, a mandatory One-Time Password (OTP) will be enabled for all Swedish players. What does this mean for you? Each time you log in, you will receive a unique OTP to your registered email or mobile number.

This additional security measure is designed to protect your account and ensure your gaming experience remains safe and secure.

If you already use a form of multi-factor authentication—such as the Stars Account PIN or RSA Security Token—this change will not affect your login process. However, please note that both the PIN and RSA Token options will no longer be accessible via the Account menu.

If you have any questions or need assistance with this new feature, our Customer Support team is here to help.
