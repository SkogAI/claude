We chatted with Matt Graham, CEO of RapidDev <https://www.rapidevelopers.com/> 
and winner of Bubble's 2025 Agency of the Year award. In five years, he's had a 
front-row seat to hundreds of product launches — and in this episode, he shares 
what that taught him about why founders stall, what the ones who break through 
tend to have in common, and how to think about building something that lasts. 



What makes his perspective a bit unusual is the path he took to get here. 
Nuclear engineer. Navy instructor. Harvard MBA. Two co-founded startups, one of 
which became a $1.5B unicorn, and then — somewhat accidentally — an agency 
that's now 220+ people strong.



There's also a candid conversation about walking away from that unicorn 
opportunity and why he doesn't regret it — which says a lot about how he thinks 
about risk and the kind of company he's chosen to build.
