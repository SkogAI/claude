What's New in 1.8

🎉 Multi-page website support

Your Solo website https://soloist.ai/designer can grow along with your business with multi-page support for 6 additional pages. Click the “Add Page” button in the top toolbar to begin. A Header navigation link will be added automatically. 

🧩 Integrations

Offer your website in another language, allow customers to leave comments, accept charitable donations, and more. Visit our Featured Integrations https://soloist.ai/integrations page to learn how web popular plugins can connect with your Solo website.
