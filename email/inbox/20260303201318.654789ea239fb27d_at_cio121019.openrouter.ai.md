Hi,

Starting Friday, March 6, we will begin collecting Value Added Tax (VAT) and any other applicable sales taxes for customers whose billing address is in the European Union.

Applicable VAT or sales tax will be automatically calculated and added to credit purchases. This change also applies to any configured Auto Top Up purchases. Taxes will be itemized on invoices and receipts.

If you're purchasing on behalf of a business, you can add a valid EU VAT ID or tax ID to your billing details to avoid being charged VAT.

To update your billing information or add your VAT ID:

* Go to your Credits dashboard ( https://openrouter.ai/credits?utm_campaign=EU+Sales+Tax&utm_content=EU+Sales+Tax&utm_medium=email_action&utm_source=customer.io )
* Click on History to open your billing portal
* Select Update information and add your VAT ID there

For details, you can view all invoices and payment history anytime in your
Credits dashboard ( https://openrouter.ai/credits?utm_campaign=EU+Sales+Tax&utm_content=EU+Sales+Tax&utm_medium=email_action&utm_source=customer.io ).

If you believe your tax treatment was incorrect, please reach out to us with relevant documentation at
support@openrouter.ai ( support@openrouter.ai?utm_campaign=EU+Sales+Tax&utm_content=EU+Sales+Tax&utm_medium=email_action&utm_source=customer.io ).
