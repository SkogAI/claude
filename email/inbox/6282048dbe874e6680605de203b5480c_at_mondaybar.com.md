Monday Bar Newsletter week 17 In this newsletter: MondayBar: The Grand 2025 Cruise, May 2-4 (no 62) • The cruise is fully booked.. but we released 4 extra cabins today • All floors and set times presented. • Don’t forget to bring a valid ID. Passport or National ID only for NON nordic citizens. • Make sure to complete your booking with all names! • Book your dinner, breakfast, brunch now and avoid waiting list • Board the ship from 15.15 (Swedish time). Pre-party on Sundeck (deck 12). Departure 16.45. • Bring your own music.. but not too big. Nothing that needs wheels… • Dress theme words: Circus - Tivoli - Funfair - Merry-go-Round • "No 62” Cruise t-shirts and MB hand fans at C.U.M. Clubwear shop. Bring cash! Dear Monday Bar family, The cruise is sold out, but today we released 4 more A-class cabins. Book here: https://se.tallink.com/monday-bar <https://se.tallink.com/monday-bar> There is also still a possibility that those of you who have booked a A or B cabin for only 2 people could upgrade to 3- or 4. Please call the booking office and find out if this is possible. Call 08-666 33 33 and have your booking reference ready. YOU NEED A VALID ID CARD OR DRIVER LICENCE TO BOARD THE SHIP - NON-NORDIC CITIZENS NEED PASSPORT OR NATIONAL ID-CARD - To get onboard, you need to prove your age and identity with a valid ID or diver’s license. If you are a NON Nordic citizen you need a passport or National ID card. Valid identification for Nordic citizens: - Regular ID card - Driver license - Passport - National ID card Valid identification for NON Nordic citizens: - Passport - National ID card Book your dinner now to avoid waiting list (cheaper) + make sure all passengers are registered! Tallink-Silja has asked us to remind all to pre-book your meals (dinner, breakfast, brunch). That way you will be assured that you can have dinner at the best time for you. With this many people on board the restaurants will fill up quickly and there will be a waiting list if you haven't pre-booked. It’s also important that all persons staying in your cabin are registered! Please contact the booking department with your booking reference handy. There’s also a chat on the Tallink-Silja homepage. Tallink Silja booking office: +46 (0)8-666 33 33 info.sweden@tallinksilja.com <mailto:info.sweden@tallinksilja.com> You can also reserve tables online. Have your booking reference ready: In Swedish: https://se.tallink.com/hitta-resa <https://se.tallink.com/hitta-resa> In English: https://en.tallink.com/en/book-a-cruise <https://en.tallink.com/en/book-a-cruise> Restaurants onboard: Swedish: https://www.tallink.com/sv/ombord/restauranger-och-barer?from=sto&to=hel&ship=silja-symphony <https://www.tallink.com/sv/ombord/restauranger-och-barer?from=sto&to=hel&ship=silja-symphony> English: https://www.tallink.com/on-board/buffet-restaurants-bars?from=hel&to=sto&ship=silja-symphony <https://www.tallink.com/on-board/buffet-restaurants-bars?from=hel&to=sto&ship=silja-symphony> BOARD THE SHIP FROM 15.15 Boarding starts from 15.15. Last time to board is 16.45 and cruise then leaves at 16.45 sharp. But the party already starts at 15.15 at Sundeck, deck 12. If the weather is bad, we’re in New York Club, deck 12-13. IT’S FREE TO CHECK IN WITH THE MACHINES. But it costs 5 Euro to checkin manually at the checkin counters. Here’s info how to get to the ferry terminal (Tallink-Silja website). In Swedish: https://se.tallink.com/vartahamnen <https://se.tallink.com/vartahamnen> In English: https://en.tallink.com/stockholm-vartahamnen <https://en.tallink.com/stockholm-vartahamnen> PARKING CLOSE TO THE FERRY TERMINAL There’s a car park next to the ferry terminal. But make sure you are in good time since the traffic in Stockholm can be congested Friday afternoon. More parking info online: https://se.tallink.com/vartahamnen <https://se.tallink.com/vartahamnen> BRING YOUR OWN MUSIC MACHINE - BUT NOT TOO BIG It’s allowed to play music in the cabins, but not too loud so that it disturbs you, neighbours. You can bring a music player, but not too big. Smaller Bluetooth speakers, computer speakers, and smaller ”boom boxes” are ok, but anything that requires wheels to carry onboard will be confiscated when boarding. If it feels too big, it probably is.. And no music in the cabins are allowed in the cabins between 06-10 so that we all can get some rest! CRUISE T-SHIRTS + MONDAY BAR HAND FANS - CASH ONLY There will be a limited number of t-shirts with the cruise logo to buy on board, in C.U.M's shop which is open from 22-01 both evenings. There will be men’s sizes from M-3XL and they cost SEK 300/EUR30. Black t-shirt with round neck. There’s also a Monday Bar Hand Fan on sale for SEK100/EUR10. BRING CASH - YOU CAN FIND AN ATM MACHINE AT THE FERRY TERMINAL AND ONBOARDThe ship has a cash machine (EUR only) close to the Information, deck 7. But we can’t guarantee that it works. There’s also a cash machine (both SEK and EUR) at the ferry terminal in Stockholm. Dress Theme inspirational words: FUNFAIR, CIRCUS, MERRY-GO-ROUND, TIVOLI There’s no limit to what some people will do with a dress theme. Our dress theme this time are these inspirational words: FUNFAIR, CIRCUS, MERRY-GO-ROUND, TIVOLI. And Dominika at C.U.M. is of course willing to help those of you who need some inspiration. LINEUP & SET TIMES Here’s the complete lineup and set times - with music styles. A good idea is to save this image on your phone! All times on the cruise are Finnish +1 hour, so forward your watch/phone 1 hour when boarding the ship, and keep it that way for the duration of the cruise. A good advice is to set the time setting on manual so the time doesn’t change when entering a different time zone. MONDAY BAR - THE GRAND 2025 CRUISE (no 62) Friday-Sunday, May 2-4 (40 hr cruise) M/S Silja Symphony Stockholm-Helsinki-Stockholm Cruise times Departure, Stockholm Tallink-Silja ferry terminal, Värtahamnen Friday, May 2, 16:45 (4:45 PM), local Swedish time Arrival, Helsinki Olympia ferry terminal Saturday, May 3, 10:30 (10.30 AM), local Finnish time (+1 hour) Departure, Helsinki Olympia ferry terminal Saturday, May 3, 17:00 (5 PM), local Finnish time (+1 hour) Arrival, Stockholm Tallink-Silja ferry terminal, Värtahamnen Sunday, May 4, 10:00 (10:00 AM), local Swedish time Age limit: 18 years when boarding.
