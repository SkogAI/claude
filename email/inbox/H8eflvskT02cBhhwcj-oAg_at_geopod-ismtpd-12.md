Hello,

Even terminal gurus spend time searching the web for complex commands. With IDE-style autocomplete, you can get work done without leaving the terminal.

Enable *AI-powered autocomplete* in Termius. It works in a local terminal and SSH sessions and needs no setup or server-side installation. Here's how you can use it to boost your productivity:

🔎 Search through the *global history* of your commands. When connected to a brand-new instance, you can find commands executed in the past on other hosts.

🎫 Paste *sudo passwords*. Autocomplete can simplify your life, allowing you to save and enter a sudo password whenever prompted.

📝 *Autocomplete commands* and their options. Termius supports over six hundred popular CLI tools, so you can easily find the correct option without scrolling through endless "man" pages.

📂 Complete filenames and paths. Navigate faster with a visual representation of your filesystem when performing cd, cp, or any other filepath-related command.
