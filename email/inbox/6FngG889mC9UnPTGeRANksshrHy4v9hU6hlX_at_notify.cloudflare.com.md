Save your 2FA backup codes now

You have two-factor authentication (2FA) enabled on your account. To prevent being locked out, we recommend securely storing your backup codes, which are the only way to recover access if you ever lose your 2FA device.

Tips for storing backup codes:

- Save them in a password manager
- Store them in an encrypted file
- Print and keep them in a secure location

If you've already saved your backup codes, no action is needed. If you've lost your codes, you can generate a new set at:
https://dash.cloudflare.com/profile/access-management/authentication/two-factor

Resources:

- Two-factor authentication: https://developers.cloudflare.com/fundamentals/user-profiles/2fa/
- Account recovery: https://developers.cloudflare.com/fundamentals/user-profiles/account-recovery/
