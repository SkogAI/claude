<h4>The app for people who actually do what they said they’d do</h4>
<p>You know that feeling when you leave a meeting and immediately forget half of what you agreed to? That’s not a memory problem. It’s a meetings problem. When you’re back-to-back all day, there’s no time to process. No time to write the follow-up. No time to turn “we should probably...” into something that actually happens.</p>
<p>Granola helps you become the person who actually does what they said they’d do. You take notes during the meeting : just quick bullets, nothing formal. Granola transcribes in the background and turns those notes into clear summaries with actual next steps. After the call, you can share your notes with the team so everyone’s aligned. Or chat with them to pull out exactly what you need to do next, without re-reading the whole thing. No more “wait, what did we decide?” moments. No more dropping the ball because you had three calls in a row and couldn’t keep track. Just clarity. And follow-through.</p>
