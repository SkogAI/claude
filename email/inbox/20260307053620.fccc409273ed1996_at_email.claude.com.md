How Claude fits into your personal life 





Life assistant 
Let Claude tackle recurring household planning tasks that eat up your time. 

Try: “I’m tired of meal-prepping the same 5 meals. Can you suggest some 
unconventional ideas that are easy to prepare but packed with protein?” 





Personal learning coach 
Claude can help you acquire new skills or knowledge more efficiently. 

Try: “I want to learn basic Spanish for my upcoming trip. Create a two-week 
study plan with daily 15-minute lessons.” 





Decision-making support 
Facing complex personal choices? Claude can organize information and help you 
think through options.

Try: “Help me compare Portugal and Switzerland for an upcoming vacation based 
on culture, cost, and travel logistics.” 





Creative project partner 
Whether planning celebrations or pursuing hobbies, Claude can spark ideas and 
help with execution.

Try: “I need creative ideas for my parents' 40th anniversary celebration that 
won't break the bank.”

Try: “Break down my car insurance policy so I understand what each section 
means.”
