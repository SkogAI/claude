Give Agents Contextual Knowledge with RAG

Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is a common technique for ensuring your agent refers to relevant and current information. Ultravox Realtime provides built-in support for custom knowledge bases (we call them "corpora"). You can create corpora in Ultravox by importing information from external data sources like product documentation, user guides, FAQs, or transcripts from training videos.
