Good news: our new Organizations for account management feature is now generally available for all DigitalOcean customers! This feature allows you to structure your accounts into a more logical hierarchy, making it easier than ever to manage access for different Teams and environments at scale.

With this new account layer, you can easily:

- Centralize your management: Manage teams, roles and memberships in one hub.
- Simplify billing: Gain visibility into spend by team or project while only dealing with one invoice and payment–no more wasted time reconciling multiple accounts.
- Set up scalable access controls: With this new account layer, you can scale smoothly as your organization grows.

You can start using Organizations today. Please visit our [product documentation](http://docs.digitalocean.com/platform/organizations) to learn more about this feature.
