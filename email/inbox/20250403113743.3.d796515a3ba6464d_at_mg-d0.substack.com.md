How Euphoria Pulled In $5,700 In 30 Days (And You Can Too)
Affiliate marketing’s dirty secret? It’s simpler than influencers [ https://substack.com/redirect/4d13e1ab-bac3-463e-bb5d-9857ee98de6f?j=eyJ1IjoiNHFkNTF0In0.3VeGxV4zk4PWCIHpd0Onx-O6RwY6Z7JeNCNSiNEVLgU ] make it sound. Euphoria’s approach skips the usual song-and-dance of building a personal brand or amassing followers. Instead, she treats it like a straightforward sales job: find hungry customers, pitch them a product they already want, and collect a cut.
Here’s the crash course.
The 10-Second Affiliate Marketing Primer
Think of affiliate links as digital commission agreements. Every time someone buys through your unique link (provided by the brand), you earn money. No inventory, no customer service, no product development — just matchmaking between buyers and sellers.
“You’re not here to become TikTok famous. You’re here to move products.” 
The Two-Part Money Machine
First, she targets “high-ticket” affiliate programs [ https://substack.com/redirect/05e218d9-545f-4fee-9b91-1a5428ed90c9?j=eyJ1IjoiNHFkNTF0In0.3VeGxV4zk4PWCIHpd0Onx-O6RwY6Z7JeNCNSiNEVLgU ] — deals that pay $30 to $200 per sale, not the pocket-change commissions most beginners chase. Think software subscriptions, coaching programs, or premium memberships. 
“You’re doing the same work either way,” she says. “Might as well get paid 10x more per customer.”
Second, she creates multiple social media accounts. Instead of one generic account, she builds multiple theme pages on TikTok, Instagram, and YouTube, all laser-focused on a single, profitable niche [ https://substack.com/redirect/26690e0b-d8f3-4b7e-87cc-d826a979fc9b?j=eyJ1IjoiNHFkNTF0In0.3VeGxV4zk4PWCIHpd0Onx-O6RwY6Z7JeNCNSiNEVLgU ] (hers: money and motivation). 
Each page acts like a 24/7 salesperson, posting bite-sized videos pairing stock footage (ocean waves, city timelapses) with punchy quotes like “Rich people do these 3 things daily.”
No face, no voiceovers, no complex edits. 
“The algorithm doesn’t care about you. It cares about stopping scrolls. A sunset clip plus a get-rich-quick quote? That stops thumbs.”
The Exact Step By Step Tutorial That You Can Follow
Step 1: The Goldilocks Niche Test
Forget “follow your dreams.” Euphoria’s niche formula is three cold, mathematical questions:
Are people already spending money here? (See: weight loss supplements, dating apps, crypto courses)
Can you find 100+ viral-ready quotes/clips about it? (Think “Five signs you’re vitamin deficient” or “Why rich people wake up at 4 AM”)
Are there affiliate programs paying $30+ per sale?
“If it’s not hitting all three, move on,” she says. Cooking fails? Too broad. Underwater basket weaving? No payout. Her sweet spot: niches where anxious buyers impulse-purchase solutions (self-help, finance hacks, beauty shortcuts).
Step 2: Affiliate Links — But Make Them Greedy
Euphoria avoids rookie mistakes like promoting $8 ebooks. 
While Amazon pays 1-10% commissions, she targets products where that percentage is $30+ (think air fryers and espresso machines). For bigger scores, she uses ClickBank—a platform packed with dubious (but lucrative) offers.
The Amazon Associates hack
Search for products with 4+ stars and 1,000+ reviews (social proof matters)
Filter for items priced over $300
Grab affiliate links for 10-20 products
Test which ones convert in her TikTok bios
“You’re not a guru — you’re a vending machine,” she says. “Stock the snacks people crave when they’re scrolling at midnight.”
ClickBank: The High-Reward, Low-Effort Play
Unlike Amazon’s modest commissions, ClickBank specializes in digital products [ https://substack.com/redirect/48f18f88-a6e1-43ea-88b9-ec4646bed358?j=eyJ1IjoiNHFkNTF0In0.3VeGxV4zk4PWCIHpd0Onx-O6RwY6Z7JeNCNSiNEVLgU ] with payouts that can hit $100+ per sale. Euphoria’s method is straightforward:
Sign up for ClickBank – No approval process, just an email and password.
Search the Affiliate Marketplace – She sticks to evergreen categories like health, weight loss, and personal development.
Pick products with a high "Gravity" score (ClickBank’s metric for popularity among affiliates).
Grab the affiliate link – No negotiation, no waiting—just copy and paste.
"The key is finding offers that sell themselves," she says. "If the sales page looks like a late-night infomercial, that’s usually a good sign."
Step 3: Linktree – The Silent Sales Funnel
Once she has her affiliate links, Euphoria doesn’t spam them—she funnels them [ https://substack.com/redirect/bd083716-28f9-44da-bfcf-698bc8f09553?j=eyJ1IjoiNHFkNTF0In0.3VeGxV4zk4PWCIHpd0Onx-O6RwY6Z7JeNCNSiNEVLgU ] through a Linktree (made via Beacons). This single link sits in every social media bio, acting as a hidden storefront.
No website needed – Just a free Linktree page with her top 3-5 affiliate offers.
Track clicks for free – Lets her see which links convert best.
Swap offers anytime – If one product stops performing, she replaces it in seconds.
Step 4: Traffic Without the Headache
Most beginners waste time on ads or complicated funnels. Euphoria skips all that and goes straight for organic traffic [ https://substack.com/redirect/ab798b3a-62b0-4fbd-ac9f-cb944bf52a7f?j=eyJ1IjoiNHFkNTF0In0.3VeGxV4zk4PWCIHpd0Onx-O6RwY6Z7JeNCNSiNEVLgU ]; algorithm-friendly content.
Her traffic strategy in three steps:
Post short, viral-style clips (no face, no voice—just text overlays and stock footage).
Use hooks like "This trick made me 3,000 in a week" to stop scrollers.
Drop a simple CTA ("Link in bio for the exact method") to drive clicks.
"You don’t need 100K followers," she says. "You just need the right 1,000 people to see your link."
The Theme Page Playbook
Instead of one personal account, she runs multiple anonymous pages (four in her case) focused on a single niche - money motivation. Each operates like its own media property on TikTok:
No original content needed - Just repurposed viral formats with fresh text
1-3 posts daily per account - The minimum threshold to stay in TikTok's good graces
All videos follow the same template:
Stock footage (cityscapes, luxury cars)
Bold white text overlays ("This mindset made me $10,000")
Trending audio (usually instrumental beats)
Use TikTok for Affiliate Marketing
Use Canva to make the videos.
Search for TikTok templates within Canva.
Look for viral clips in the niche you’re going with and make similar ones.
Match viral trends using cityscapes, luxury cars and similar stock videos, as well as motivational quotes
"TikTok rewards consistency, not creativity. My top-performing video took 90 seconds to make in Canva."
The New Account Advantage
Euphoria swears by fresh accounts over established ones. Here's why:
First 5 videos determine your reach - She spends days perfecting these before going live
New accounts get an initial boost - TikTok tests virgin profiles with extra impressions
No shadowban history - Clean slate means full algorithmic benefits
[image]
The 1,000 Follower Hurdle
TikTok blocks affiliate links until you hit 1k followers. Euphoria's workaround:
Use "Link in bio" CTAs from day one to train viewers
Cross-promote between accounts once one takes off
Leverage Instagram simultaneously (no follower minimum for links)
"The first thousand is the hardest," she admits. "Then it's just copying what already worked 100 more times."
Take Away
When reflecting Euphoria’s approach, she runs on anonymity rather than authenticity. Her playbook strips social media to its bones: platforms need endless content, users crave quick dopamine hits, and neither party cares if a person or a bot delivers it. 
This surely isn’t glamorous. I guess you could say it’s a clinical repeat of what algorithms reward. Post, track, cash out. Rinse until the platform catches on, then rebuild.
