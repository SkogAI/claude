Identity-Consistent Video Gen: Hunyuan Custom ( https://www.runpod.io/console/deploy )

**********************************************************************************************
Identity-Consistent Video Gen: Hunyuan Custom ( https://huggingface.co/tencent/HunyuanCustom )
**********************************************************************************************

Multimodal video generation just leveled up.

Tencent’s Hunyuan Custom ( https://huggingface.co/tencent/HunyuanCustom ) model can generate short videos that preserve facial identity, character consistency, and motion realism—and it’s now available on Hugging Face. These models require serious GPU muscle (80 GB VRAM+), making them a perfect showcase for RunPod’s A100 and H100 multi-GPU pods ( https://www.runpod.io/console/deploy ).

***************
Why it matters:
***************

*
Works best with ≥8x A100s or 4x H100s
* Ideal for animation, AI avatars, and visual storytelling
* Open weights mean you can test and build fast

*******************
How to get started:
*******************

*
Choose “Pods” from the left menu
* Select A100s or H100s
* Slide the slider to 8 or 4 GPUs, respectively
* Click "Deploy On-Demand" at the bottom
Spin Up Hunyuan Custom Now ( https://www.runpod.io/console/deploy )

INTELLECT-2: First 32B RL LLM via Permissionless Swarm ( https://x.com/PrimeIntellect/status/1921730059620196772 )

------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
INTELLECT-2: First 32B RL LLM via Permissionless Swarm ( https://x.com/PrimeIntellect/status/1921730059620196772 )
------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Trained through asynchronous reinforcement learning by a decentralized contributor base, INTELLECT-2 ( https://www.primeintellect.ai/blog/intellect-2-release ) is the first of its kind. Both code and weights are public. RunPod is the ideal playground to launch it across multiple nodes ( https://www.runpod.io/console/cluster/create ) and show how decentralized RL scales.

***************
Why It matters:
***************

*
Shows what’s possible with decentralized, permissionless model training
* Proof-of-concept for swarm-based reinforcement learning at scale
* A prime candidate to run across RunPod’s multi-node clusters ( https://www.runpod.io/console/cluster/create ) for live inference or retraining
*
Open weights and code = instant demo material and infrastructure showcase

DeerFlow: Your AI Researcher That Reads, Writes, and Talks ( https://github.com/bytedance/deer-flow )

Perfect fit for “always-on” RunPod Pods ( https://www.runpod.io/console/deploy ) or Serverless ( https://www.runpod.io/console/serverless )—lets users spin up a full analyst in one click
