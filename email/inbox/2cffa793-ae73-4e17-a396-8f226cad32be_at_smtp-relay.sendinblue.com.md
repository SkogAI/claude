**Complete your GPT Workspace installation**
============================================


Here is how to open the addon:

 


- First go to your [Google Sheets](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets), [Slides](https://docs.google.com/presentation) or [Docs](https://docs.google.com/document) 
 


- Then click on the **Extensions menu**, the click on the **GPT for Docs Sheets Slides** option and finally click on **Start**.


 

After clicking on Start you should see a sidebar open on the right side of your screen. 

 

[Try it now in Google Spreadsheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/create)
