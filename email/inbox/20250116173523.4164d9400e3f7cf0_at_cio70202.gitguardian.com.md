Happy new year to all you developers and security experts! 🎊 

We built 2024 around your feedback and the challenges you face. From much-anticipated product updates to major innovations, our mission remains clear: to help you secure your systems and secrets with complete confidence and control.

The numbers tell a part of our journey—
- 13 Million Secrets Detected (Public GitHub)
- 500k Users Secured

- 75 Platform Updates
- 25 Detector Engine Versions
- 15 New Sources
- 13 ggshield Releases

 

Ultimately the real story is yours: you shape what we build.

Let’s celebrate the major milestones we achieved together this year.
