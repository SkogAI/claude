On September 20th, Redeye will host Gaming Day 2022 at SPACE - the world’s greatest gaming center!

On this day, we gather industry professionals, professional/retail investors, gaming students, and passionate gamers to explore the future of gaming. This day will be filled with panel discussions from company representatives from all verticals and platforms within the industry. Topics and trends that will be discussed are VR, Esports and buzzwords such as Metaverse and NFT. We will also discuss more business-critical questions, such as “How to create moats in mobile gaming” and “Is the pure premium business model dying”?
