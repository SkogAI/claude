MONDAY BAR: THE 2025 FAMILY CRUISE, NOV 7-8 • 240 amazing pictures by Timo Torvikoski Dear Monday Bar family, We just had an amazing cruise on M/S Birka Gotland, a shorter 21 hr cruise that works better for a lot of people with work, family planning, etc. And we just put up 240 new cruise pictures by our own excellent photo guy, Timo Torvikoski. https://www.mondaybar.com/photos So now it’s time to present our next cruise, Monday Bar: The 2026 Summer Cruise, June 12-14. The first Summer cruise in 7 years! A long standing tradition with a daytime deck party and night time dancing under the stars. This will be the only 2-night cruise during 2026. One problem for many theme cruises is that when the tickets are released many 4 person cabins are blocked by bookings for only 1-2 people. The booking system will take from 4-person cabins when the 2 person cabins run out. And that is a big problem since we need to max the number of guests to make ends meet. And the party is way more merry with max party people on board! So this time we will start by first releasing only 4-person cabins that can be booked for 3-4 persons (not less). And when we feel that need is filled we will release the remaining 2 person cabins. But no later than January 26. If you feel you’d like to know more about this, there’s a discussion going in our Facebook group where we try to explain as well as we can. https://www.facebook.com/groups/mondaybar Monday Bar crew 🥰
