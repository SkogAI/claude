<h2>Alignment</h2>
<p><strong>Writers have an edge in AI. </strong>I built something with Claude Code recently that has no right to exist. I can’t code. But somehow I now have a website that scrapes academic papers on GLP-1s, summarizes them for busy doctors, grades the evidence, and emails subscribers weekly. I’ve checked it every day for the past three weeks, and it works. </p>
<p>I created the site out of pure frustration. I can’t keep up with all the research, and rather than saving hundreds of papers on my desktop, I needed something simple and easy to understand. </p>
<p>There are two reasons I could make this. One is that writing for Every means I’m constantly reading about how these tools actually work from people at the frontier like <strong>Dan</strong>, <strong>Kieran</strong> and <strong><u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiQ2xhdWRlIENvZGUgVGFrZXMgUG9sZSBQb3NpdGlvbiIsInBvc3RfaWQiOjM4OTgsInBvc3RfdHlwZSI6InBvc3QiLCJ1cmwiOiJodHRwczovL3d3dy5tb25vbG9ndWUudG8vIiwicG9zaXRpb24iOjI4fQ==" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Monologue</a></u></strong> general manager <strong><u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiQ2xhdWRlIENvZGUgVGFrZXMgUG9sZSBQb3NpdGlvbiIsInBvc3RfaWQiOjM4OTgsInBvc3RfdHlwZSI6InBvc3QiLCJ1cmwiOiJodHRwczovL2V2ZXJ5LnRvL0BuYXZlZW5fNjgwNCIsInBvc2l0aW9uIjoyOX0=" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Naveen Naidu</a></u></strong>. The other is I know how to take a vague idea and make it specific, which is all essay writing really is.</p>
<p>First, I asked Claude to interview me in depth. What do I want people to feel when they go to my site? What are the flaws? What am I missing? This is a technique I use in my writing to gain clarity about what I want to say. I then traced the logical flow on the page: If I’m collecting academic papers, I need a way to identify them so they’re not posting to the site more than once. If I’m emailing people, I need a way to track what’s already been sent, and If I want papers to be easy to follow, I need a way to surface the evidence clearly.</p>
<p>What started as a simple idea required, it turns out, a pretty sophisticated system. But that’s how essay writing works, too. You think you’re making one point, then realize you need to define a term first, or address an objection, or split one argument into three.</p>
<p>By jotting down what I wanted step by step, I could see gaps I would have missed, and gaps I didn’t want Claude to fill with assumptions or hallucinations because it would inevitably get them wrong. I wanted to catch these early potholes myself.</p>
<p>I think that’s the edge right now: the habit of writing your way to clarity before asking anyone or anything to execute. Writers have been doing this forever, but it’s especially useful now.—<em><u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiQ2xhdWRlIENvZGUgVGFrZXMgUG9sZSBQb3NpdGlvbiIsInBvc3RfaWQiOjM4OTgsInBvc3RfdHlwZSI6InBvc3QiLCJ1cmwiOiJodHRwczovL3d3dy5nbHAxZGlnZXN0LmNvbS8iLCJwb3NpdGlvbiI6MzB9" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Ashwin Sharma</a></u></em></p>
