Hello Emil,

We’re writing to inform you that as of January 30, 2026, the Dataplex  
Catalog[1] will fully replace the legacy Data Catalog[2] service. As a  
result, the legacy Data Catalog will be discontinued. This will require you  
to migrate from the legacy Data Catalog to the Dataplex Catalog.

As part of our transition to a unified data and AI platform, Dataplex  
Catalog will become an integral part of BigQuery. This consolidation  
streamlines data engineering, governance, and AI capabilities into a single  
experience, simplifying operations and accelerating your data initiatives.

We’ve provided additional information below to guide you through this  
change.

What you need to know

The Dataplex Catalog, now generally available[3], provides a unified  
inventory of your metadata across Google Cloud and on-premises resources,  
with enhanced features and improvements in comparison to the legacy Data  
Catalog capabilities.

All data sources available in the legacy Data Catalog are supported by the  
Dataplex Catalog, along with data search and exploration. New tools[4]  
enable metadata migration from Data Catalog to Dataplex Catalog. Once  
migrated, resources are exclusively managed in Dataplex Catalog and are no  
longer active in Data Catalog.

What you need to do

Before January 30, 2026

    - Transition your Data Catalog users, workloads, and content to Dataplex  
Catalog.
    - Follow the steps as described in the Transition from Data Catalog to  
Dataplex Catalog[5] guide.

Related changes in Catalog and other Google Cloud services

Effective now

    - Dataplex Catalog supports certain functionalities differently to Data  
Catalog. To learn more please visit the comparison between Dataplex Catalog  
and Data Catalog[6] and features that aren't supported in Dataplex  
Catalog[7].
    - Data Lineage shows entry details[8] from Dataplex Catalog.

Effective Q2 2025 (some required actions if you use SDP or Business  
Glossary)

    - In Sensitive Data Protection (SDP)[9], outputs from the discovery  
action “Send to Dataplex as tags” will be made available in Dataplex  
Catalog as aspects under a new aspect type. Learn more about the transition  
from Data Catalog to Dataplex Catalog[10]. This discovery action will stop  
functioning by September 30, 2025.
       - A new action “Send to Dataplex as aspects” will become available  
from Q2, 2025 and you must activate it to continue bringing Sensitive Data  
Protection scan outputs to Dataplex Catalog.
       - The inspection job action “Publish to Dataplex“ will stop  
functioning by September 30, 2025.
    - The Business Glossary, currently in preview, will be supported in  
Dataplex Catalog in general availability from Q2 2025. From this launch,  
you will be able to view and modify association of terms to entries or  
search entries by terms in Dataplex Catalog.

Effective September 30, 2025

    - Ingestion of Dataplex entities[11] (including those created as output  
of Dataplex Discovery[12]) will stop functioning by September 30, 2025.  
External tables in BigQuery published by Dataplex Discovery will be  
ingested into Dataplex Catalog as entries.
