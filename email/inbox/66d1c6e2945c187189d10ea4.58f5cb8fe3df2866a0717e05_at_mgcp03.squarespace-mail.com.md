Monday Bar Newsletter week 35 In this newsletter: MONDAY BAR: THE 2024 CRUISE, SEPTEMBER 20-22 • A few more cabins were released today, both 2- and 4 person cabins. • Please send in info on all passengers in your cabin • Theme: Welcome to the Jungle - dress to impress! • Book your dinner, breakfast, brunch now and avoid waiting list • Bring your music box.. but not too big. Nothing that needs wheels… • Important: You need a valid Passport or National ID to board the cruise (not regular ID or driver license) Dear Monday Bar family - here's some important info if you are going on the cruise! The cruise has been sold out since May. But today a few more cabins were released from the cargo department. Both 2- and 4-bed cabins. If you’re quick you can grab one. https://se.tallink.com/monday-bar <https://se.tallink.com/monday-bar> There is still also a possibility that those of you who have booked an A, B or E cabin for only 2 people could upgrade to 3- or 4 persons. Please call the booking office and find out if this is possible. Call 08-666 33 33 and have your booking reference ready. Send in info on all passengers It’s important that you send in all passengers in your cabin; name, birthdate, nationality. Please call the booking department with your booking reference handy or send an email: info.sweden@tallinksilja.com <mailto:info.sweden@tallinksilja.com> There’s also a chat on Tallink-Silja's homepage. Tallink Silja booking office: +46 (0)8-666 33 33 Reminder about Passport and National ID!! We’d like to remind you to bring either a valid Passport or a National ID Card to the cruise to prove your identity and what country you are from. Regular ID or Drivers License are NOT valid identification. And please check the expiration date. Dress theme: WELCOME TO THE JUNGLE! There’s no limit to what some people will do with a dress theme. Or perhaps just dress in green with a some plants and birds.. And Dominika at C.U.M. is of course willing to help those of you who need some inspiration. Book your dinner now to avoid waiting list (also cheaper) Tallink-Silja has asked us to remind all to pre-book your meals (dinner, breakfast, brunch). That way you will be assured that you can have dinner at the best time for you. With this many people on board the restaurants will fill up quickly and there will be a waiting list if you haven't pre-booked. Please call the booking department with your booking reference handy or send an email: info.sweden@tallinksilja.com <mailto:info.sweden@tallinksilja.com> There’s also a chat on Tallink-Silja's homepage. Tallink Silja booking office: +46 (0)8-666 33 33 You can also reserve tables online. Have your booking reference ready: In Swedish: https://se.tallink.com/hitta-resa <https://se.tallink.com/hitta-resa> In English: https://en.tallink.com/en/book-a-cruise <https://en.tallink.com/en/book-a-cruise> Restaurants onboard: In Swedish: https://se.tallink.com/baltic-queen#tabs-content-3 <https://se.tallink.com/baltic-queen#tabs-content-3> In English: https://en.tallink.com/en/baltic-queen-tallink-cruise-ship-stockholm-tallinn#tabs-content-2 <https://en.tallink.com/en/baltic-queen-tallink-cruise-ship-stockholm-tallinn#tabs-content-2> Bring your own music, but not too big It’s allowed to play music in the cabins, but not too loud so that it disturbs your neighbours. No music is allowed between 06-10 in the morning. You can bring a music player, but not too big. Computer speakers, ”boom boxes” are ok, but anything that requires wheels to carry onboard will be confiscated when boarding. If it feels too big, it probably is..
