Finally, a proper presentation mode! ( https://x.com/randyjhunt/status/2024494538711056394?utm_campaign=What%27s+New+3.4+-+Part+1+-+English+only&utm_campaignid=3157&utm_campaigntype=newsletter&utm_content=What%27s+New+3.4&utm_medium=email_action&utm_source=customer.io )

Presentation mode started in a very Notion way: Randy and Cole
on our design team woke up one day and decided, "presenting a Notion page should be easy… and fun." So they built it.
Now, you can present directly from a page without having to rebuild your work. Try it with the shortcut ⌘ + opt + p on Mac, or Ctrl + alt + p on Windows.

It's currently in beta for everyone on
Plus, Business, and Enterprise. Check out Randy's demo ( https://x.com/randyjhunt/status/2024494538711056394?utm_campaign=What%27s+New+3.4+-+Part+1+-+English+only&utm_campaignid=3157&utm_campaigntype=newsletter&utm_content=What%27s+New+3.4&utm_medium=email_action&utm_source=customer.io ).

**************************************

* H4
headers: Smaller than H3, bigger than text ( https://x.com/NotionHQ/status/2034319575345774830?utm_campaign=What%27s+New+3.4+-+Part+1+-+English+only&utm_campaignid=3157&utm_campaigntype=newsletter&utm_content=What%27s+New+3.4&utm_medium=email_action&utm_source=customer.io ).

improvements: Integrations are now easier to build and more reliable, with automatic retries, Markdown and Meeting Notes support, and clearer setup warnings, so workflows break less often and ship faster.
