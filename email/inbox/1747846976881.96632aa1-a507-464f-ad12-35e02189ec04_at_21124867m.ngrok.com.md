## Protect endpoints with mTLS and a minute of your time

I'm also happy to announce a long-awaited update to Traffic Policy: the terminate-tls action is live, and lets you bring your own certs, route traffic based on TLS handshake details, and wire up mTLS with an unquestionably simpler process than any other gateway you've ever used.

```
on_tcp_connect:
  - actions:
    - type: terminate-tls
      config:
        mutual_tls_certificate_authorities:
          - |-
          -----BEGIN CERTIFICATE-----
          ... certificate ...
          -----END CERTIFICATE-----
```

We didn't just stop there—you can now use more granular user agent variables (https://d2v8tf04.na1.hubspotlinks.com/Ctc/5F+113/d2v8tf04/VWwJ_05WYG0bW2XcbtV8bdly6V7Wb0N5wSqPtN46-8Kv3pyd0W95jsWP6lZ3n6W5CbSJm20M-2WN3W7TxGZQm35W17MH4d5DWTfgW8mKmjZ4bdNTRW2pJRcm7tTy9tW26Jz0B5KWcNxW1Y7tRH5_bGR4W6TWwmH90Bc68W2R9hNq71_83cW1x-GWl7cFXKxW298fl42xvZCsW3VZwR31PDWZMW3pcXtf5zDyDkN9jYKYQTNxhbN3QPmY_FmLxwW71-SH46DQTD3W8vKJXj3zHg4SW4mRdY92WsVcQW3kNRGv4bFkbZW74zsbr9cDHqBW36JJP72C40_rW3XK8FP8hLj8lW2_spwB7MQY9KW6B3QtT1-5w8CW87Gs6J3_1BLMW2x2K9F5Vf_yBW2xsc3d6c0F-5W22Lstf6t9rn4W7-8TG052MN67W1w5JgK2k7BrXf21mhSg04 ) to take action on traffic based on who's pinging your endpoint. ```

```
  - expressions:
      - "req.user_agent.is_bot"
    actions:
      - type: deny
```
