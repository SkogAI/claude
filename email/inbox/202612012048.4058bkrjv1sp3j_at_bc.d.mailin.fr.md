*"You can't connect the dots looking forward; you can only connect them looking backwards. So you have to trust that the dots will somehow connect in your future."*- Steve Jobs


Today, that future has arrived.


We're excited to introduce **Graph View 2.0,** a visual, interactive representation of how everything you save in Recall connects. Your notes, articles, podcasts, videos, and books come alive as a dynamic web of knowledge.

What is a Graph View?
---------------------

Every time you save content to Recall, we automatically extract connections and link it to what's already in your knowledge base. Graph View visualizes those connections, showing you how your ideas relate in ways you might never have noticed.


*Note: you can also create unlimited personal notes, connect them and explore your graph on the free plan.*
