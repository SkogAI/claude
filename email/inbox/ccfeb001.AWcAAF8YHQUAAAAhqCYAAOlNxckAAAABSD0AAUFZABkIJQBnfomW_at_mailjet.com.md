January 2025 - New Login, SCIM 2.0 and Open Q&A
Happy New Year and January, Emil

A cheery seasonal thank-you for staying informed through our Product newsletter where our Zitadel team keeps you informed about the latest updates around platform feature releases, community happenings, online events, and insights on helpful approaches on simplifying and getting the most out of your identity infrastructure.



Highlights
With a new year, there are some exciting new features and updates to share from the Zitadel crew for January:

 * New Typescript Login experience including OIDC [https://zitadel.com/docs/guides/integrate/login-ui/typescript-repo] launches for self-hosted customers enables admins to enhance their Zitadel instance with a ready-to-deploy login that is highly customizable. 
 * We have started implementing SCIM (System for Cross-domain Identity Management), the standard for inbound user-provisioning so other systems can automatically provision and manage users within Zitadel. We will be adding new endpoints over the next two months. Interested in test flighting this feature? Ping Jim@Zitadel.com [jim@zitadel.com] to join in on the fun.

Zitadel Adopters
Sharing experiences and learning from each other is essential.  This helps our end users to share their use cases and  get in touch with peers to share experiences and best practices.

We created a user-maintained directory of adopters [https://github.com/zitadel/zitadel/blob/main/ADOPTERS.md] to help identify practitioners of core features. As you use Zitadel, please consider adding yourself as a user with a quick description of your use case by opening a pull request to this file and adding a section describing your usage of Zitadel.
