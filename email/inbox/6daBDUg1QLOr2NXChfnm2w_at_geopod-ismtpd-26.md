Termius Redesign is Now Complete
--------------------------------

Following the recent updates to our Desktop ( https://termius.com/blog/termius-x ) and iOS ( https://termius.com/blog/termius-for-ios-new-navigation-and-sftp ) apps, we’ve brought a new major update to Android—a final milestone in the redesign of Termius!

This release introduces a UI upgrade designed to make navigation smoother and bring a desktop-class experience to phones and tablets.

* *On phones* , there is a new bottom tab bar that provides quicker access to your Vault, Connections, SFTP, and Settings.
* *On tablets* , you can enjoy a desktop-like experience with a tab bar at the top, which allows you to easily access your Vault and active connections at any time.
* *On foldables* , the UI is optimized to make the most of the larger screen when open and smoothly transitions to a compact view when folded.

And here's our main driver to push the limits and create a desktop-class SSH experience for mobile devices.

Autocomplete: Passwords, Command Options, and Snippets
------------------------------------------------------

Autocomplete just got even better! Alongside the existing history and path completions, we’ve added:

* *Password suggestions* for sudo, MySQL, or other commands that prompt for credentials.
* *Command options* , arguments, and their descriptions, just like in man pages.
* *Snippets* – your favorite commands that you can now run from autocomplete.

Try autocomplete and tell us what you'd love and what we can improve. *Join Discord* ( https://discord.gg/KpZw78pRcF ) to share feedback.
