*Workspace Update*
------------------

In December, we introduced *Workspaces* , a feature designed to help you organize and group multiple connections within a single tab. It comes with two modes: *Split View* and *Focus Mode*. Now, with our latest update, you can save Workspace templates, making it easier than ever to pick up right where you left off.

Save Workspace Templates

Getting started is simple: just *drag and drop* one tab over another to create a Workspace. To save it as a template, click the *Workspace tab icon*. Whenever you add or remove tabs, the same icon will appear, allowing you to save the changes.

Restore Workspaces

You can find your *saved* *templates* in the *New Tab* screen. Simply double-click on a template to launch a new Workspace. The New Tab also displays recent connections, which you can quickly convert into a Workspace template with the *Create Workspace* button.

We're actively improving Workspaces, and more updates are on the way. Have feedback or feature requests? Join the discussion on our dedicated Discord channel! ( https://discord.gg/MP8twZu2FR )

*Ansible* *Integration*
-----------------------

In dynamic environments where infrastructure changes quickly and provisioning happens automatically, keeping vaults up to date is challenging. As new machines are spun up or shut down, you need to create or delete hosts in your vaults to ensure they reflect the latest infrastructure changes. Such manual updates often result in errors, and cleaning up unused hosts takes a lot of time.

We introduce *Termius integration with Ansible* ; it allows you to automate the import of hosts and groups with a simple playbook, ensuring your vault always reflects your latest infrastructure changes.

Curious to see it in action? Read the blog ( https://termius.com/blog/termius-integration-with-ansible ) to learn how to streamline host creation with Ansible.
