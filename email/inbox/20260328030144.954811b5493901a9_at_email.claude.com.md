A proactive assistant that works on your behalf


Claude Code can now work on your behalf while you’re away—using your tools and 
your computer, running tasks on a schedule, and notifying you on the Claude 
mobile app when something’s done or needs your go ahead.

 <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=fVIV-L49eBs> 

 * Work from your phone. With Dispatch 
<https://code.claude.com/docs/en/desktop?utm_source=it&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=2026_Q1_Dispatch_Computer_Use_Launch_on_CC&utm_term=claude_code&campaignId=17409921&utm_content=inline_link#sessions-from-dispatch>
, you can kick off a build, run tests, or put up a PR from your phone, and 
Claude executes it on your local machine. You get pinged when it's done. 
 * Want more control? Use remote control 
<https://code.claude.com/docs/en/remote-control?utm_source=it&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=2026_Q1_Dispatch_Computer_Use_Launch_on_CC&utm_term=claude_code&campaignId=17409921&utm_content=inline_link#sessions-from-dispatch>
 to start a session in your terminal, then pick it up from the web or the 
Claude mobile app. Withchannels 
<https://code.claude.com/docs/en/channels?utm_source=it&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=2026_Q1_Dispatch_Computer_Use_Launch_on_CC&utm_term=claude_code&campaignId=17409921&utm_content=inline_link#sessions-from-dispatch>
, you can control your Claude Code session through select MCP servers, starting 
with iMessage, Telegram, and Discord. 
 * Run tasks on a schedule. Use /loop to have Claude run recurring tasks 
<https://code.claude.com/docs/en/scheduled-tasks?utm_source=it&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=2026_Q1_Dispatch_Computer_Use_Launch_on_CC&utm_term=claude_code&campaignId=17409921&utm_content=inline_link#sessions-from-dispatch>
 on a schedule—a morning PR review, a nightly test suite—in the CLI, on the 
desktop app, or on the web. 
 * Let Claude use your computer. Let Claude use your computer 
<https://code.claude.com/docs/en/desktop?utm_source=it&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=2026_Q1_Dispatch_Computer_Use_Launch_on_CC&utm_term=claude_code&campaignId=17409921&utm_content=inline_link#let-claude-use-your-computer>
 to complete tasks via the desktop app. Claude reaches for the most precise 
tool first (usually your CLI and relevant connectors). When there isn't one, it 
can control your browser and screen directly. 
 * Claude will ask permission before accessing new applications, and you can 
stop Claude from using your computer at any point. Safeguards against prompt 
injection are built in. 

To get started: Make sure your desktop is set to stay awake—Claude needs it to 
be on to access your tools and logged-in apps. Pair your desktop app with 
mobile, and try handing off a task.



Download the Claude apps 
<https://claude.com/download?utm_source=it&utm_medium=email&utm_campaign=2026_Q1_Dispatch_Computer_Use_Launch_on_CC&utm_term=claude_all&campaignId=17409921&utm_content=hero_cta>

Computer use and Dispatch are available on Pro and Max plans. Channels are 
available on all paid subscription plans. These features are research previews. 
Complex tasks may take more than one try, and we're sharing these features 
early to learn where they work and where to push them further.


Dispatch works on all platforms that support the Claude desktop and mobile 
apps, but computer use requires macOS and must beenabled in the app settings 
<https://claude.ai/desktop/settings/desktop>.
