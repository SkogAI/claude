We've spent recent months connecting Claude to everything you use—your 
calendar, documents, and daily tools—so it can collaborate on more types of 
work. Now, we're taking the next step: bringing Claude directly to your browser.



We believe browser-using AI is inevitable. But it brings serious safety and 
security challenges that we need to solve properly.



So we’re starting with an experiment. We’ve built Claude for Chrome, where you 
can chat with Claude in a sidebar, and have it take actions on your behalf 
within your browser window.



 <https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-for-chrome>



What Claude can do in Chrome: 

 * Manage your calendar and email
 * Handle routine tasks like data entry or repetitive forms
 * Test websites by navigating user flows
We’re piloting this with a select group of Max users to gather real-world data 
on use cases, limitations, and safety issues, before expanding access. Browser 
AI tools are already emerging across the ecosystem, many of which are built on 
Claude’s models, making safety improvements in this area urgent.



Important: Browser use is experimental technology with real risks. We’ve built 
permission controls and safeguards, but start with trusted sites and supervise 
Claude carefully.Learn more. <https://www.anthropic.com/news/claude-for-chrome>
