###Build. Preview. Deploy. All Inside Windsurf.


 

**Wave 6 is here**—and it’s headlined by a massive new capability: **Deploys**. For the first time, you can build, preview, and now deploy an app—all from the Windsurf Editor.
 


What's new in Wave 6?
---------------------


###Deploys: From Local to Live


Previews in Wave 4 gave us local iteration. **Deploys** now completes the full lifecycle. You can now deploy your app — built in Windsurf — to a public URL via Netlify with a single click.

 

Just click the **Deploy rocket icon** or ask Cascade. View build history, claim your deployment, and share it instantly.

 


- Free users: 1 new site/day 
- Paid users: 2 new sites/day 
- All users: 10 deploys/hour


**Commit Message Generation: Your Diff, Summarized**
----------------------------------------------------


No more writing commit messages by hand. Click once, and Cascade generates a smart summary of your changes. (*Available to all paid users.)*


**Cascade Navigation: Rewind Easily, Stay in Flow**
---------------------------------------------------


When Cascade goes off track, don’t waste time scrolling. The new **Table of Contents overlay** makes it easy to:


- Jump back to any part of a conversation 
- Revert to earlier steps with a click 
 

And we’ve done some behind-the-scenes magic too. Using smart checkpointing and summarization, Cascade now performs better in **long conversations**—so response quality holds steady even as chats get complex.


**Enterprise Features: Now with Admin Controls**
------------------------------------------------


Enterprises can now access powerful features like:


- **Model Context Protocol (MCP)** 
- **Turbo Mode + command execution** 
- **Allowed models configuration** 
 

Control everything from your [admin portal](https://codeium.com/team/windsurf_settings). 


###Windsurf Tab Upgrades


We launched Tab in Wave 5, but that announcement was only the beginning. In Wave 6, we have added context of a user’s search to Tab, allowing for delightful moments.

 


- Uses your **search** context to suggest better completions 
- Full **Jupyter Notebook** support 
- Faster, smarter **tab-to-jump** 
- UI speedups and quality-of-life fixes


**Other Major Updates Since Wave 5**
------------------------------------


 

**Wave 6 is packed with behind-the-scenes polish:**
 


1. New Windsurf Editor app icons to choose from 
2. Improved **MCP compatibility** with support for **SSE-based services**. 
3. Better **Memories** retrieval 
4. Persistent bug fixes 
5. Latest **VS Code** upstream changes merged in 
6. Ability to edit suggested terminal commands before Cascade runs them
