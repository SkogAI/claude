We're rolling out exciting new features for Apollo Connectors — our solution that lets you build GraphQL APIs without writing a server by directly connecting your frontend to any REST or GraphQL backend. Connectors eliminates thousands of lines of boilerplate code, and our community forum is where you'll see the newest capabilities first.
Just shipped: N+1 batch loading support for Connectors
If you've ever watched your application fire dozens (or hundreds) of individual REST requests when fetching related data, you know the pain of N+1 queries. Our new batch loading support automatically combines these requests, dramatically improving performance without requiring you to rewrite your data fetching logic.
