Traffic Policy on Kubernetes can now read pod identity at connection time. With v0.22.1 of the ngrok Operator, policies can enforce access based on not just IP addresses, but pod name, namespace, UID, and annotations. If a compromised pod in namespace-a tries to reach a service in namespace-b, the connection gets rejected before it touches your upstream.
