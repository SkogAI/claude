What's new


Getting started on mobile is now easier than ever. When you create a new app 
on Bubble, you can choose to generate a web app, a mobile app, or start from 
scratch. All powered by Claude’s state of the art frontend generation model, 
Opus 4.5.



Want to build for mobile? Web? Both? Bubble is the only platform where you can 
do all three without code, switching between them as your idea evolves. And 
with Bubble AI you can get a head start on native mobile apps the same way you 
can with web apps.



The AI creates your app's design with native mobile UI patterns, adds sample 
data so you can see how it works, and sets up dynamic expressions that connect 
your interface and database.



Speed your builds, stay in control


Once the AI generates your app, you can use Bubble's visual editor to refine 
it exactly how you want. If you need to make changes, jump in and edit directly 
without waiting on AI to figure things out.



This means less time on prototypes and more time building features that matter 
to your users and business. The combination of AI speed and visual editing lets 
you scale from your first user to thousands or even millions without losing 
that control.



The Agent's prompt-to-edit capabilities for mobile (where you can ask it to 
directly modify your mobile app) will be in beta by Q1 2026, so keep an eye out 
for updates.
