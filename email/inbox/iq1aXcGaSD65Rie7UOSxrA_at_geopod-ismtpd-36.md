🪟 Broadcast commands in Split View

Remember the last time you did the same update on 10 servers one by one? With split view and broadcast, you can update similar servers simultaneously. Drag-n-drop up to 16 terminals next to each other and hit Cmd + B (Mac) or Ctrl + Alt + B (Win and Linux). Voila! You're in the broadcast mode. Whatever you type will be sent to all terminals simultaneously, allowing you to complete your task much faster.

🗄️ Single source of truth that works

Managing your infrastructure as a team can be challenging when the information is scattered across spreadsheets, wikis, and password managers. Team vaults in Termius are designed to be your team's single source of truth. Vaults allow you to set granular permissions, ensuring only the right team members have access. Explore how vaults can help your business in our recent blog post ( https://termius.com/blog/single-source-of-truth-that-works ).
