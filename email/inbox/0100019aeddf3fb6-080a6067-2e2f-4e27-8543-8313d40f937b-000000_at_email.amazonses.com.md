Highlights of the Week✨

 
Evaluation Alignment
Last week we launched automatic evaluation generation from your annotations. This week, we’re taking it a step further.
To ensure evaluations reflect your criteria (or the domain expert’s), we now test them against your ground-truth annotations and
regenerate them until they are aligned to your judgement.
 



 
 
Annotation Progress Bar
As we’ve said before (and will keep repeating), annotations are the foundation of a strong Reliability Loop.
Your feedback on whether the model’s output is correct or not is what allows the system to improve.
To help with this, we’ve added a progress bar that encourages you to complete at least 10 annotations (though we still recommend
aiming for more, ideally 100+).
Once those first 10 are done, the Issues panel unlocks, giving you visibility into where your prompt is failing.
 



 
 
More Info on Models and Providers
When selecting a model for your prompt, you’ll now see key details right next to each model, including cost per million tokens,
context window, and capabilities like reasoning or tool calling. This makes choosing the right model much faster and easier.
