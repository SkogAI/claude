Once again, it’s time for the annual Redeye Gaming Event. With two years of Covid-regulations behind us, we want to celebrate by creating a massive real-life event at SPACE, the mecca of gaming. We have gathered institutional investors, industry experts, and important participants in the gaming ecosystem, creating a melting pot of talent, experience, and innovation.

Discussions will cover the takeoff of VR, User Generated Hype, and the continued adoption of Esports. We are also happy to welcome the Nordic Game Discovery Contest, the Gaming Industry’s equivalent of Dragons’ Den.
