************************
Untap, Upkeep, Open Beta
************************

We are happy to officially announce the start date of Open Beta
on September 27, at 1:00PM PDT/20:00 UTC. At this time, the
"Gates" will be thrown open and players will no longer need a
Closed Beta key to access Magic: The Gathering Arena.

**************
Save the Dates
**************

* September 21, 8AM PDT - The in-game store is turned off (no
more purchases until Open Beta).
* September 24, 8AM PDT - MTG Arena and the official forums go
offline. Export your decklists beforehand.
* September 27, 1PM PDT - Open Beta Starts!
During the downtime, we will be wiping all accounts as well as
archiving the Closed Beta forums

*************************
Open Beta Welcome Weekend
*************************

On September 27, as a thank you for all the bugs reported and
all the games played in Closed Beta, anyone who had created an
account during the Closed Beta will get an email with a code that
redeems Masterpiece copies of these three Planeswalker cards in
MTG Arena:

* 1x Ral, Izzet Viceroy
* 1x Teferi, Hero of Dominaria
* 1x Vraska, Golgari Queen
We'll also be hosting a special Singleton Event with no entry
fee as soon as the Open Beta is live! Build a deck with only a
single copy of any Standard legal card, and compete against other
players for prizes to help kick start your collection.
