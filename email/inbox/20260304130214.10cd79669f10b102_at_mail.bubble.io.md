Episode 4 of The New Build just dropped! 🎤


 Meet Brian Henderson. He grew up helping his dad run a laundromat in Broken 
Arrow, Oklahoma — and ended up using all his industry knowledge to spot a gap 
in the market and build a multi-million dollar software company that now serves 
laundromat owners across all 50 states even though he has no coding background.


 Brian started out reselling someone else's point-of-sale software before 
realizing he needed to build his own. Two competitors he met at a laundromat 
trade show had each spent six figures on custom development and had nothing to 
show for it, which was all the confirmation he needed to build his own system.


 You'll pick up practical lessons on building for a niche market, shipping 
software in a physical-world business, and why iteration with real customers 
beats planning theperfect product in isolation — all wrapped in a story that 
ends with Brian buying back the family laundromats where it all began.
