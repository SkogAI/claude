Monday Bar Newsletter week 36 In this newsletter: MONDAY BAR CRUISE No 60 JUBILEE, 6-8 OCTOBER, 2023 • 2 night cruise to Tallinn, Estonia • Dress Theme: Colour Explosion! • Cabins are SOLD OUT - but you can still join. Read how. • Reminder: Don’t forget to bring a valid Passport or National ID Dear Monday Bar family, All cabins are now SOLD OUT, but there is still capacity to fill booked cabins with more people (up to 4 persons per cabin in standard A, B and E class). So if you know someone who have booked only 1-2 persons in one cabin, they should be able to get an upgrade for an extra fee. You should speak to the booking office about this and have your booking reference ready. Call +46-8-666 33 33 or send an email to: kundservice@tallinksilja.com Dress theme: COLOUR EXPLOSION People have asked if we will have a dress theme at The Last Cruise in October. The previous theme was Black & White, so now we're doing the opposite, COLOUR EXPLOSION!! Our decor will of course be adapted accordingly. And Dominika at C.U.M. is of course willing to help those of you who want some inspiration. Reminder about passport and National ID!! We’d like to remind you to bring either a valid Passport or a National ID Card to the cruise to prove your identity and what country you are from. Regular ID or Drivers License are NOT valid identification. And please check the expiration date. Last night at Kolingsborg - January 5, 2014 Someone put out this clip on YouTube from the last Monday Bar night at Kolingsborg January 5, 2014 before it was demolished. This was also the the very last night at this legendary venue that opened 60 years earlier. Were you there? https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=IW6q8A9agL8 Monday Bar crew 🥰 Who would have guessed over 20 years ago that we would sail together for the 60th time on the Baltic sea! We’ll, here we are, and it’s time again to board the best party ship of the Tallink-Silja fleet, M/S Baltic Queen. The cruise is in the beginning of October, but thanks to an outside sun deck partly covered with a roof and wind shield we should be able to enjoy a daytime party on deck while in port in Tallinn on Saturday. MONDAY BAR CRUISE No 60 JUBILEE - THE LAST CRUISE Friday-Sunday October 6-8, 2023 (2 nights - 41h) M/S Baltic Queen Stockholm-Tallinn-Stockholm Cruise times Departure, Stockholm Tallink-Silja ferry terminal, Värtahamnen Friday, October 6, 17:30 (5:30 PM), local Swedish time Arrival, Tallinn Tallink D-terminal Saturday October 7, 10:45 (10:45 AM), local Estonian time (+1 hour) Departure, Tallinn Tallink D-terminal Saturday October 7, 18:00 (6 PM), local Estonian time (+1 hour) Arrival, Stockholm Tallink-Silja ferry terminal, Värtahamnen Sunday October 8, 10:30 (10:30 AM), local Swedish time Age limit: 18 years when boarding.
