Welcome to playbooks, the community for AI app builders.

Hey, it's Ian ( https://x.com/iannuttall ) —founder of playbooks. Welcome!

I use AI to build apps for both fun and profit, mostly in Cursor,
and share things I learn along the way.

playbooks is my attempt to build a community of people who love
to build with AI - whether you're a seasoned pro developer, or
just getting started with vibe coding.

To get started, here's what you should check out first:

* Build your Cursor rules 
( https://kodama.playbooks.com/proxy/tracking/emails/erUt08J5X4wxU5R6VdF8rBZo29Ygns3w/click?signature=46dcaa8f8d7e74ed80f9e0e9fe893811badd513c&url=https%3A%2F%2Fplaybooks.com%2Frules%3Futm_source%3Dbento%26utm_medium%3Demail%26utm_campaign%3Dbroadcast%26bento_uuid%3Dafd6dcba-8dc4-4b4e-a11d-7dcb0a6f052f )
 - this is a different take from the usual. The idea is that as
the AI models progress we don't need to provide so much context
in rules. Keep them concise, focused, and modular. Then you can
add your own unique rules to them later without wasting valuable
context tokens.

* Learn how to use AI 
( https://kodama.playbooks.com/proxy/tracking/emails/erUt08J5X4wxU5R6VdF8rBZo29Ygns3w/click?signature=ce54e2c52fe5a6eef9ff1cd95c5510f4a1c42c0e&url=https%3A%2F%2Fplaybooks.com%2Flearn%3Futm_source%3Dbento%26utm_medium%3Demail%26utm_campaign%3Dbroadcast%26bento_uuid%3Dafd6dcba-8dc4-4b4e-a11d-7dcb0a6f052f )
 - instead of courses that take a long time and most of us never
finish, I'm going to share bite-sized tutorials you can quickly
watch/read. Get the gist and then go learn how to code with AI by
coding with AI!

* Improve your workflow with MCPs 
( https://kodama.playbooks.com/proxy/tracking/emails/erUt08J5X4wxU5R6VdF8rBZo29Ygns3w/click?signature=28e2515ce641feceaa8ab2b96da642ad53d0d2bf&url=https%3A%2F%2Fplaybooks.com%2Fmcp%3Futm_source%3Dbento%26utm_medium%3Demail%26utm_campaign%3Dbroadcast%26bento_uuid%3Dafd6dcba-8dc4-4b4e-a11d-7dcb0a6f052f )
 - search the growing directory of MCP servers with easy to
follow instructions on how to use them to streamline your process
and help AI agents do more for you.

* Connect with the community - also coming soon is a simple
little community to chat and connect and share ideas. X is great
for this, but there's a lot of FOMO and overwhelm so I want to
build a smaller, calm community we can all learn from.

If you have questions, feedback, or suggestions, pleas hit reply
or send me a message @iannuttall ( https://x.com/iannuttall ).
