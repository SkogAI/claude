Here’s the gist:

**v3.25 - Three New Features That Work Together**
We've built three interlocking systems that solve the biggest challenge in AI coding: keeping agents on track through long, complex tasks.

**1. Deep Planning (/deep-planning)** - A four-step workflow: (1) silent codebase investigation, (2) targeted questions, (3) comprehensive implementation_plan.md, then (4) fresh task handoff. Eliminates context pollution by separating planning from execution.


**2. Focus Chain** - Persistent todo lists that travel with your task from the beginning to the end. Every 6 messages, the to-do list is re-injected into context. It's a north star that cuts through accumulating context noise.


**3. Auto Compact** - Automatic context summarization when approaching limits. A task requiring 5M tokens can complete in a 200k context window. Never lose context again.


The result? Cline can tackle problems that would derail other agents at turn 50. Read more about it here: https://cline.bot/blog/cline-v3-25

 

**Also Available: “Sonic”, a new stealth model**
We’re excited to introduce *Sonic*, a new stealth model designed for coding. It comes with a 262k context window and is **free to use in Cline** during its alpha period. Your usage helps refine the model in this early stage, so every task you run contributes to its progress.

Read the announcement: https://cline.bot/blog/new-stealth-model-in-cline-sonic
