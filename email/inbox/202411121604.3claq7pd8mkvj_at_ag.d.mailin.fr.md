NetBird for Android v0.0.27

- What’s included:

Persist route selection [(#2810)](https://az5n7.r.a.d.sendibm1.com/mk/cl/f/sh/1t6Af4OiGsFxg9YTXuco2XdXjMYIMg/vsSdGfxxhD7r)

- What it does: Saves your route selection preferences across restarts of your NetBird client or computer, ensuring the configuration persists (except on iOS and Android).
- Why you’ll love it: No more reconfiguring overlapping route selections after a restart! This fix addresses issues like maintaining custom route preferences and ensures a seamless user experiencefor managing complex networks.

👉[Learn more](https://az5n7.r.a.d.sendibm1.com/mk/cl/f/sh/1t6Af4OiGsGQ0rktwGijCnhN7BX24k/0wYFFmq_lLNb)

Account different policy rules for route firewall rules [(#2939)](https://az5n7.r.a.d.sendibm1.com/mk/cl/f/sh/1t6Af4OiGsGsLZxKKcoeN3lCV0Vlmo/TCu7lcZ0X2XT)

- What it does: Ensures that route firewall rules generation considers the source group peers defined in access control policies, applying rules specifically to these peers. Traffic from distribution group peers not belonging to any source group will be dropped.
- Why you’ll love it: This improvement resolves a flaw where multiple policies with different access levels were incorrectly applied to all distribution group peers, ensuring more precise and secure policy enforcement. Existing valid policies benefiting from this flaw may experience behavior changes.
