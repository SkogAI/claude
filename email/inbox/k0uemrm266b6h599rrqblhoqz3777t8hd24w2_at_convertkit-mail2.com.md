Here’s what to expect –

* Zero setup, instant debug – Get debugging in seconds, not
hours, with minimal setup and full flexibility when you want it.
* Clean UI that adapts – Appears when needed with a clean UI.
Customize views to match your debugging style.
* Never leave your keyboard – Navigate sessions, set breakpoints,
and inspect variables without ever reaching for the mouse.

⌘⇧D, debugger: start or click the Debug Panel (bug icon) in the
status bar to get started and take a look at our docs (

) for more detailed configuration help.
