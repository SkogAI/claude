We are writing to inform you of a billing error that affected your account and 
the steps we have taken to resolve it.



What Happened

As a member of our Development Partner Program, you received a 30% discount on 
eligible Claude Code API usage. Unfortunately, due to an error in our billing 
system, you were inadvertently charged twice for your eligible Claude Code API 
usage:

 * Once at the standard rate
 * Once at the discounted rate
This resulted in you being charged 170% of the standard rate instead of the 
intended 70% (reflecting your 30% discount).



What We've Done

 * Our billing partner has corrected the system configuration to prevent this 
error going forward
 *  We have issued a credit card reimbursement for the overcharge on all your 
previous Claude Code usage since enrolling in the Development Partner Program
 * Example: If you spent $170 on eligible Claude Code API usage since 
enrollment in the Development Partner Program, you have been refunded $100, 
which should be viewable on your credit card statement within 5-10 business days
To acknowledge this inconvenience, we have added an additional $20 credit to 
your account.



Next Steps

The refunds have already been processed and should appear on your credit card 
statement within 5-10 business days. If you need additional support, please 
contact us atsupport.anthropic.com <http://support.anthropic.com>.



Our Commitment

We sincerely apologize for this billing error and any confusion it may have 
caused. We are committed to providing transparent, accurate billing for all our
