Intelligent planning mode

Intelligent Planning Mode ( http://lovable.dev )

Chat mode has been upgraded to plan mode. Lovable now:

*
Asks thoughtful questions to make sure it understands everything you need
*
Creates detailed, editable plans for you to approve
*
Shows exactly what it will build at every step without losing the plot

The result? You can hand off more complex builds while you answer emails, grab a coffee, or finally teach your dog to high-five like a pro.

Prompt queuing

Prompt Queuing ( http://lovable.dev )

Delegate multiple tasks to Lovable and step away. Now you can:

* Stack multiple prompts
* Re-prioritize tasks as you go
* Collaborate on projects without waiting for your turn

= less waiting, more shipping.

Automated testing with browser tools

Automated Testing ( http://lovable.dev )

Lovable now tests your apps like a real user, and fixes them like an experienced developer. It can:

* Explore your app in a browser, including filling out forms and clicking through menus
* Test different flows end-to-end
* Catch bugs and fix problems on the spot

These automated quality checks mean you review production-ready work, not broken drafts. Just ask Lovable to check everything works.
