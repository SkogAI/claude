Hello Emil,

We’re excited to announce that Pinecone Assistant is now generally available (GA) for all users!

Developers of all levels have already built thousands of AI assistants, accelerating time to value across diverse use cases like financial analysis, legal discovery, compliance, and more. Now, with GA, it’s easier than ever to launch knowledgeable chat and agent-based applications in just minutes.

Here's what’s new:

- Optimized interfaces with new chat and context APIs powering chat and agent-based applications
- Custom instructions to tailor your assistant’s behavior and responses to specific use cases or requirements
- New input and output formats with added support for JSON, .md, and .docx files in addition to PDF and .txt
- Region control with options to build in the EU or US
