NEW SUPPRESSED STATUS

https://resend.com/changelog/email-suppression-visibility

A suppression happens when Resend intentionally prevents an email from being sent to a recipient. This occurs when deliveries to that address have bounced or been marked as spam. We introduced a new suppressed status to give you clearer visibility into your email performance and help you respond with confidence.

→ Read the changelog https://resend.com/changelog/email-suppression-visibility
