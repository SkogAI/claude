Scale GenAI Apps Like InstaHeadshots ( https://blog.runpod.io/instaheadshots-scaling-ai-generated-portraits-with-runpod/ )
**************************************************************************************************************************

InstaHeadshots transformed casual selfies into over 15 million studio-quality headshots — without building a massive infrastructure team. By leveraging RunPod's Pods ( https://www.runpod.io/console/deploy ) for training and Serverless ( https://www.runpod.io/console/serverless/new-endpoint ) for inference, they scaled rapidly while keeping costs low and performance high.

Facing fluctuating demand and diverse GPU workloads, they needed a solution that could adapt in real-time. RunPod's flexible infrastructure allowed them to auto-scale resources and select the optimal hardware for each task, all while paying only for what they used.

The result? A seamless migration completed in a single day, a 50% reduction in GPU costs, and a 100% improvement in performance — empowering them to focus entirely on growth and product development.

***************
Why it matters:
***************

* Successfully delivered 15M+ AI-generated headshots using RunPod's infrastructure
*
Combined Pods ( https://www.runpod.io/console/deploy ) and Serverless ( https://www.runpod.io/console/serverless/new-endpoint ) for efficient training and inference workflows
* Achieved significant cost savings and performance gains
*
Demonstrates how lean GenAI teams can scale real-time AI applications effectively
