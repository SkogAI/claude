A few weeks ago, we released Edit Prediction (
https://click.convertkit-mail2.com/92u4zwzl33cnh6xz2vdf9hzelg333sw/g3hnh5hmd6kqrzfr/aHR0cHM6Ly96ZWQuZGV2L2Jsb2cvZWRpdC1wcmVkaWN0aW9uP2U9NDg=
), Zed's native prediction mechanism, powered by Zeta, our new
open source, open data model. Lots of people love it as-is, but a
common piece of feedback has been that predicted text appearing
as you type can feel distracting—or even "too in-your-face."

We heard that feedback, and are excited to share our new subtle
mode (
https://click.convertkit-mail2.com/92u4zwzl33cnh6xz2vdf9hzelg333sw/9qhzhnhdqzl50gt9/aHR0cHM6Ly96ZWQuZGV2L2Jsb2cvb3V0LW9mLXlvdXItZmFjZS1haT9lPTQ4
) for Edit Prediction. This produces predicted text that's hidden
by default, and only visible when you're holding a modifier key.
That keeps it out-of-your-face, but easily accessible at a
moment's notice.

With Zed, we want to offer you productivity-enhancing AI-assisted
coding capabilities, without breaking your the editor flow state
you're used to. We believe Edit Prediction's subtle mode meets
that mark, and the initial feedback is that it's minimal,
elegant, and helpful, but we'd love to hear your thoughts and
feedback!. You can read more about the feature here (
https://click.convertkit-mail2.com/92u4zwzl33cnh6xz2vdf9hzelg333sw/9qhzhnhdqzl50gt9/aHR0cHM6Ly96ZWQuZGV2L2Jsb2cvb3V0LW9mLXlvdXItZmFjZS1haT9lPTQ4
