We are very proud to announce Aya Vision (https://email.cohere.ai/e3t/Ctc/2N+113/d2t4Dg04/VWrBdY6X3bf9N4fYTbRpx_FyW5l3Nw35s-vn6N3fD2Cg3l5QzW5BW0B06lZ3nqW31r-ZG3b9D4dVXWgtV5Sp-m3W43ngKL8ZVtHfW8KX_nB3sRhR2W4xHjJs6xMkzrW4Yhxf_7rL94fW2bGq20164bXqW42vbZp8fHwTMW9gxJ-x61D0bWN5bBtB9c8d1hW7BtVGl5W2j1bW7ZJdYV996dvDW1ZP0PR7rXcLmW513H072KlFH3W98Z25Y4yhzCjTfcRg51rXhZW16QjpZ6Rhc7YW4Lg60d6_HPYRf1_FG2804 ) , a state-of-the-art vision model excelling across multiple languages and modalities. Our release expands multimodal capabilities to 23 languages spoken by over half the world's population.

Our Aya Vision models perform well in a variety of tasks, including image captioning, visual question answering, text generation, and translating both text and images into clear, natural-language text.

Aya Vision outperforms the leading open-weight models in multilingual text generation and image understanding.  In its parameter class, Aya Vision 8B achieves the best performance in combined multilingual multimodal tasks, outperforming Qwen2.5-VL 7B, Gemini Flash 1.5 8B, Llama-3.2 11B Vision, and Pangea 7B. Aya Vision 32B sets a new frontier in multilingual vision open-weights models, outperforming Llama-3.2 90B Vision, Molmo 72B and Qwen2-VL 72B.

Aya Vision 8B outperforms models 10x its size such as Llama-3.2 90B Vision with 63% win rates. Aya Vision 32B outperforms models more than 2x of its size, such as Llama-3.2 90B Vision, Molmo 72B, and Qwen2.5-VL 72B, with win rates ranging from 50% to 64% on AyaVisionBench and 52% to 72% on mWildVision average across 23 languages.
