Hey Emil,
 
In March, we released the Circleback CLI, made desktop recording as simple and hands-off as having Circleback join your call, and
introduced views. Here's what's new.
 


Circleback CLI

You can now search and access your meetings, emails, calendar events, and more from your terminal with the Circleback CLI
[https://circleback.ai/releases/circleback-cli]. Access everything from the command line, or give your agents the conversation
context they need to get things done.



 


Desktop recording updates

Don't want a bot in your meetings? You no longer have to compromise on experience.
With automatic start and end enabled, Circleback detects when your meeting begins and ends based on microphone activity
[https://circleback.ai/releases/automatically-end-recordings-on-microphone-use], so you don't have to think about it.



You can now also:
 * Switch your microphone or captured window [https://circleback.ai/releases/change-microphone-and-screen-while-recording]
   mid-recording

 * Choose whether to include video [https://circleback.ai/releases/choose-whether-to-record-meeting-video-on-desktop] by default

 * Change the linked calendar event [https://circleback.ai/releases/change-calendar-event-while-recording] while a recording is in
   progress

 
Enable automatic recordings from Settings → Notifications in the desktop app [https://circleback.ai/desktop] and Circleback takes
care of the rest.
 


Views

You can now save a set of filters as a view [https://circleback.ai/releases/views] and switch between views with one click. If you
find yourself filtering meetings or action items the same way over and over, save it as a view. Customer calls, hiring loops,
project action items–organize work the way you think about it.



 


Other releases

 * Circleback in ChatGPT [https://circleback.ai/releases/circleback-in-chatgpt]

 * Dark mode on mobile [https://circleback.ai/releases/dark-mode-on-mobile]

 * Improved share modal [https://circleback.ai/releases/improved-share-modal]

 * Number of invitees automation condition [https://circleback.ai/releases/number-of-invitees-automation-condition]
