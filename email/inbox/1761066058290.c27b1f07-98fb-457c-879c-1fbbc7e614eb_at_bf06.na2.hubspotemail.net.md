Thank you for being a part of the community and giving us feedback.

Over the past three years, we’ve come to believe that building reliable agents requires a new approach. We call this agent engineering — the iterative process of refining LLM systems into reliable experiences.

Since we launched langchain, we’ve heard that developers want more control, better visibility, more flexibility. So we’ve built:

- LangChain: Completely rewritten to help you get started fast with a core agent template and middleware for flexibility
- LangGraph: A lower-level framework giving you control over every step, with durable execution for long-running tasks
- LangSmith: Our platform for taking agents to production with observability, evaluation, and deployment

This week, we're launching major 1.0 releases of LangChain and LangGraph, plus new capabilities in LangSmith – including an Insights Agent for aggregating usage patterns on production data and our first no-code text-to-agent builder.
