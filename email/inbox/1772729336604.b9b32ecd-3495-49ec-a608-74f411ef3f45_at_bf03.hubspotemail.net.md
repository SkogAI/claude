Define triggers and scheduled actions inside Zenflow. Hook into commits, PR events, test results, or custom conditions. Agents handle execution from there.

Integrations

Zenflow now connects to what you're already using:

- GitHub. OAuth or PAT. Agents open branches, manage PRs, sync checks.
- Jira. Import issues directly. Agents triage, prioritize, and act on your backlog.
- Linear. Sync project management with agent automation.
- MCP Servers. Per-agent backends for extended capabilities: Context7, Playwright, custom endpoints, and more.
