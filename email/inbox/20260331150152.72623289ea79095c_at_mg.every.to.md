<p>Every’s consulting team is growing. Right now, we have two potential new hires in a trial period: Jean-Claude, who’d manage our sales pipeline, and Claudette, a visual designer.</p>
<p>You might be surprised to learn that they’re both AI agents. If they’re able to reliably do what we need them to and we bring them on full-time, our team will consist of four human and three agent employees.</p>
<p>Claudie, our first AI colleague, has been with us for two months. <strong><u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiV2hhdCBJIExlYXJuZWQgT25ib2FyZGluZyBPdXIgQUkgUHJvamVjdCBNYW5hZ2VyIiwicG9zdF9pZCI6NDA5MCwicG9zdF90eXBlIjoicG9zdCIsInVybCI6Imh0dHBzOi8vZXZlcnkudG8vQG5hdGFsaWFfMjk0NCIsInBvc2l0aW9uIjo1fQ==" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">Natalia Quintero</a></u></strong>, Every’s head of consulting, and I rely on her to <u><a href="https://every.to/emails/click/f91b1e00930c1c7802951fb76da18da9d1d86023a9ec7b653e47ca1b5d584dbf/eyJzdWJqZWN0IjoiV2hhdCBJIExlYXJuZWQgT25ib2FyZGluZyBPdXIgQUkgUHJvamVjdCBNYW5hZ2VyIiwicG9zdF9pZCI6NDA5MCwicG9zdF90eXBlIjoicG9zdCIsInVybCI6Imh0dHBzOi8vZXZlcnkudG8vcG9kY2FzdC9ldmVyeXMtaGVhZC1vZi1jb25zdWx0aW5nLWp1c3QtYXV0b21hdGVkLWhlci1qb2IiLCJwb3NpdGlvbiI6Nn0=" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">track where every client project stands</a></u> and to make sure nothing falls through the cracks, work that saves the team 15 hours per week. It’s hard to imagine operations without her.</p>
<p>Getting her up to speed, however, was neither a seamless nor a linear process. That road is paved with previous iterations of Claudie we had to fire because they were not structured right. </p>
