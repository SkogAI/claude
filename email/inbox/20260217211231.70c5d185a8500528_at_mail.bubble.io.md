YouTube first, app second: Why this approach worked for Turing App







Episode 3 of The New Build podcast just dropped!



Meet Vai Singh. He built the Turing App <https://theturingapp.com/> and grew 
its YouTube channel from zero to almost 55,000 subscribers in less than a year, 
all while working full-time as head of strategy at one of Europe's biggest 
space services firms. 🧑‍🚀



The idea came to Vai while he was on parental leave and couldn't find science 
content that struck the right balance. Everything was either too academic or 
far too simple. So he and his co-founder decided to fill that gap themselves.



You'll hear how Vai used YouTube to validate his content before building the 
app, why he chose Bubble for the build (created in just four weeks!), and his 
framework for heavy prioritization to balance his passion project and career. 
Plus, his honest take on filtering constructive feedback from trolling.
