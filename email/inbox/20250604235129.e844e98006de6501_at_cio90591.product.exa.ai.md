Hey Emil,

We’re rolling out a major improvement to Auto search in our API. The new architecture doesn’t create meaningful relevance scores for results. As a result, we’re deprecating scores in Auto search on June 18th.

If your app uses Auto search scores, we recommend migrating as soon as possible. You can read more about search types in our docs here ( https://docs.exa.ai/reference/exas-capabilities-explained#search-types ).
Note: Scores in Neural search results will remain unchanged.
Let us know if you have any questions!
