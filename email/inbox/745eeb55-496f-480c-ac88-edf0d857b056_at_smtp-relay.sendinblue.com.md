Welcome to the most realistic, versatile and contextually-aware AI audio platform, providing the ability to generate speech in hundreds of new and existing voices in 32 languages. We are thrilled to have you join ElevenLabs.

 

We've packed a lot of features into our tool to support the diverse ways our users leverage text-to-speech to create content. To help you start, we wanted to share a quick walkthrough of the platform. 

 

We recommend you follow along in the product dashboard: 
[ Launch ElevenLabs](https://elevenlabs.io/speech-synthesis)


####First, generate speech from text...


You can type (or paste) any text into the Speech Synthesis tool. A voiceover script for a video you're working on. An article. Even a poem. If you're not sure what to do, try this:


*Oh, dear user, with ElevenLabs you'll find,*
*A world of voices, both gentle and kind.*
*Text turned to speech, in languages wide,*
*Where lifelike emotions gracefully reside.*


####**Next, find your favorite voice(s)**


Navigate to the Voice Library, where you can explore hundreds of lifelike voices created by the community. You can filter by gender, age & accent to experience the wide range of voices on our platform.


####**Then, dub a video into one of 29 languages**


Next up, go to Dubbing. Paste a Youtube video link, choose your target language, and we'll do the rest. Our dubbing tool analyzes the audio, translates the speech, and synthesizes the translated text into an audio track that maintains the original voice tone and style.
