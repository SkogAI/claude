🃏 OddsMate



OddsMate <https://oddsmate.replit.app/>: Texas Hold'em rewards calculated 
risk, butcalculating is the hard part.

OddsMate handles the math so poker fans can focus on the game. Input your hand 
and the community cards, get your probability of winning, make better calls.

 Clean, focused, built for people who take their poker seriously. Thanks Ron 
<https://x.com/Ron_Ward_>!




🧥Jacket Oracle



Jacket Oracle <https://jacketoracle.com/>: Do I need a jacket today? It's the 
morning question Ialways answer incorrectly.

Jacket Oracle answers it instantly based on current weather conditions in your 
area.

 No weather apps to interpret, no second-guessing, just a straightforward 
answer when you're rushing out the door.

Delightfully specific, immediately useful, exactly what you need. Thanks for 
this one!


🏈 GamePlan



GamePlan <https://gameplan.live>: Sports streaming is so complicated that 
figuring out how to watch your own team requires a spreadsheet and a 
subscription audit.

GamePlan cuts through the noise by telling you exactly how to watch your 
favorite teams: which service has the rights, whether you're blacked out, what 
your options actually are.

Brian built this as a generalist with zero coding experience, solving the 
specific problem that only someone who's hunted through five apps for a single 
game could understand.

From the creator: "Before Replit, I would have ideas and they would live only 
in my head. Now, I have brought two projects to life in a matter of months."

 Ideas moving from "someday maybe" to "live and helping people." Thanks Brian!


🧠 Pixel Progress



Pixel Progress <https://pixelprogress.pcr.org.uk/>: Building a custom 
fundraising platform used to mean months of developer time and five-figure 
budgetsor paying a share to a third party.

Craig shipped one himself in 24 hours and raised over £10,000.

 Pixel Progress turns donation campaigns into collaborative pixel art—each 
contribution fills in the picture, creating a visual fundraising thermometer 
that donors actually want to engage with.

The constraint? They could have raised more if the grid was bigger (turns out 
success creates interesting new problems).

From the creator: "I have no coding experience, but lots of ideas! Replit is 
an incredible tool, as it allows me to quickly prototype ideas and send a 
mock-up to clients. What would have taken weeks and/or cost thousands can now 
be done in minutes."

 Thanks Craig <https://www.linkedin.com/in/craiglinton/>! 
<https://www.linkedin.com/in/craiglinton/>
