Do you ever add "Reddit" to the end of your search to avoid sponsored posts or sites full of affiliate links? Or sometimes need
search results from only trusted academic papers? We understand the need for precision in your search.

That's why we built Focus, a powerful tool that allows you to narrow your search to specific sources. Whether you're looking for
genuine feedback on Reddit, searching through billions of YouTube videos, or conducting scientific research, Focus empowers you to
find exactly what you're looking for.
 




 


How to get started with Focus


Hit the "Focus" button

When starting a new search Thread, simply click the "Focus" button located at the bottom left of the search box.


Choose your source

Select from various options like academic journals, YouTube, or Reddit.


Get focused answers, fast

With Focus, you're in control of your search—and that means more relevant and precise results than you could ever get on a
traditional search engine.
