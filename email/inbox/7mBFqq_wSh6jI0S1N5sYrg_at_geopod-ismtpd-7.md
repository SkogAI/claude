Being stuck in the terminal usually means endless ping-pong chats with command output exchange. In this email, you will see how to collaborate in the terminal.

Shared terminal sessions allow you to collaborate with contractors and colleagues in just a few clicks. Share a link or jump to a teammate's session to start collaborating. Here are a few examples of how you can use it:

▶️ *Show.* Copy a link to your session on the terminal and send it to people you want to join your terminal. It is perfect to show a problem you face to a contractor or teach your teammates new tricks.

⏺️ *Join.* On the hosts screen, you can see teammates connected to shared hosts. Join their session to see what they are up to and whether they need help.

⌨️ *Help.* Sometimes, it is faster to do it yourself. Viewers can request editing permissions to type in directly in the terminal and get things done.

Real-time collaboration requires no installation on a server; it works via Termius using a peer-to-peer WebRTC with end-to-end encrypted connection.
