3X Cheaper Egress for Cache Hits
********************************

Cached egress is egress that is served from our CDN via cache hits, and it is now 3 times less expensive.

[GitHub ( https://github.com/orgs/supabase/discussions/38119 )]

********************************
Expiring personnel access tokens
********************************

All personal access tokens can now be created with an expiration date and the usage of the token is tracked so you can remove the ones that are unused.

[Dashboard ( https://supabase.com/dashboard/account/tokens )]

***************************************
Self-service SSO for Teams + Enterprise
***************************************

Organizations on Teams and Enterprise plans can now configure SSO themselves from the Dashboard.

[Docs ( https://supabase.com/docs/guides/platform/sso )]
