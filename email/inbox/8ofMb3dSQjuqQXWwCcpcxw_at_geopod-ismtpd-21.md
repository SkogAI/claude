*🚀 Exciting New Features Coming Soon!*

We're on the verge of releasing some game-changing new features that will transform how you interact with Straico, and I wanted to share with you some sneak-peaks of them:

*Multimedia Gallery:* Finding and enjoying your creations is about to become much easier! Our new gallery view brings all your multimedia content into one place, with complete details about how each item was created and simple ways to save and reuse them.

*Contextual Chatbar:* A simplified experience with contextual menus that put every setting you need right at your fingertips - no more jumping between modals or screens! Explore all our multimedia generation capabilities (text to image, image to video, text to voice) confidently and with ease, featuring models from Flux, Runway, Ideogram, Kling, ElevenLabs, and more.

*Side by Side Context Tree:* We're enhancing our model comparison feature with a visual Context Tree that clearly shows which model is active in the conversation. Visual connecting lines help you track which model answers you've selected, making it easy to follow your conversational thread across different AI models.

*Straico Teams:* Our most requested feature is almost here! Straico Teams will enable enhanced collaboration, allowing team members to work together using all the Straico features you love. Enjoy increased efficiency with higher API limits and smart resource management that lets you allocate coins across team accounts for optimal distribution.

Know more about Teams ( http://straico.com/teams )

*When can you expect all of these great features?*

You can expect the upcoming features to be available during June, with Straico Teams arriving in July. We’re committed to delivering these enhancements promptly to improve your experience and collaboration, but as with all plans, it may change, so stay tuned in our Discord community to be among the first to get all the specifics!

Fresh out of the code-oven:*

*Straico Node for n8n:* The Straico Community Node integrates AI capabilities into your self-hosted n8n workflows. Connect your Straico account to send prompts to various models and use AI responses in your automations. Generate responses from multiple models, automate personalized emails, and enhance workflows with RAG-based agents.

Check out the n8n node! ( https://straico.com/integrations/n8n/ )

*🔧 Recent Improvements You May Have Missed*

Over the past few months, we've rolled out several significant enhancements:

*Smarter Error Messages:* We've made error messages more interactive, providing recommended alternatives and enabling instant model switching when issues occur. These improvements help you stay in flow even when models fail.

*Pricing Per Message Model:* We introduced a new pricing option allowing users to pay a flat rate per message regardless of length, making long conversations and large file handling more cost-effective. You can easily switch between pricing modes in your settings.

Which Pricing mode is best for your needs? ( https://straico.com/pricing-modes/ )

*LLM Combos:* Create, save, and seamlessly switch between customizable groups of language models tailored for specific tasks like writing, coding, or translation. Pre-built combos are available for common scenarios, streamlining your workflow.

*Image-to-Video Generation:* Powered by Runway Gen-3, you can now transform still images into dynamic short videos directly within Straico. Simply upload an image or use one generated by Ideogram, add a prompt, and customize ratio and duration.

Know more about Image to Video ( https://straico.com/runway-video-generation/ )

*API Improvements:* Several new features including image support for context in prompt inputs, image generation with Ideogram and Flux, a smarter model selector based on price and quality, options to update existing RAGs, and a prompt enhancer to improve outputs in both text and images.

*Smart Merge:* Easily combine responses from multiple AI models by dragging and dropping columns for customized merging, highlighting differences, or creating unified responses. This feature enhances workflow flexibility and delivers sharper results.

Learn about Smart Merge ( https://straico.com/smart-merge/ )

*New Models with Advanced Capabilities:* We've added a variety of new models featuring Reasoning and Deep Research functionalities, keeping Straico at the cutting edge of AI capabilities. A couple of them that you may have heard everywhere are Claude Sonnet 4, and OpenAI o3!
