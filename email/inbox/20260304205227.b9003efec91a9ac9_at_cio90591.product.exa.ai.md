Introducing Exa Deep
********************

 
Today we're launching a revamped Exa Deep ( https://x.com/ExaAILabs/status/2029277019369029800 ): a search endpoint that conducts agentic research on your behalf to provide the best possible results.

Deep automatically performs optimized query expansion and LLM reasoning to get the most out of the high-quality search results that Exa already provides:

 
Exa Deep Eval Chart
 
See how it works: https://x.com/ExaAILabs/status/2029277019369029800

 
 
 
Exa Deep is already being used by customers for detailed people & company search, financial research, and news reports. It supports custom structured outputs that act like enrichments (e.g. size of company, number of employees).

 
Exa Deep Eval Chart
 
On the inside, Deep uses our recently-launched Exa Instant search ( https://exa.ai/blog/exa-instant ) in a tree-like pattern. It reasons to understand the intent behind your query, generating multiple search agents in parallel, and synthesizing results with citations.

It's our best agentic search endpoint available.
