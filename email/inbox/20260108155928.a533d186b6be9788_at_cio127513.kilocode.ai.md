agents can use.

📋 CLI Image Paste Support
----------------------------

Paste images directly into the CLI with Ctrl+V on macOS.
Images attach as [Image #N] and get sent with your messages. A
proper visual workflow without leaving the terminal.

npm install -g @kilocode/cli
