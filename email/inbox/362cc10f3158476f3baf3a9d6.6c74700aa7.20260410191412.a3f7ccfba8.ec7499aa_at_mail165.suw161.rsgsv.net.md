** DoltLite Improves
------------------------------------------------------------

You may know there is an ongoing holy war between Vibe Code and Trad Code (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=338a08725b&e=6c74700aa7) . The frontline here at DoltHub is DoltLite (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=059511da93&e=6c74700aa7) , our entirely vibe-coded fork of SQLite with Dolt-like version control features.

This week I forced Aaron to review it (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=57bee5d0e6&e=6c74700aa7) , leading to his comment above. I had already been working hard to make DoltLite better (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=90bc57a9d3&e=6c74700aa7) , and Aaron's review sparked a couple of major releases (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=c90cd1766f&e=6c74700aa7) .

Do you want to be part of the Vibe Code revolution? Try DoltLite (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=16b1482ddd&e=6c74700aa7) ! Let's stick it to the mean Trad Coders.


** Doltgres Works with Prisma
------------------------------------------------------------

Speaking of Trad Code, we still write artisanal, small-batch software here at DoltHub: Dolt, Doltgres, and all our other products. This week we're proud to announce that Doltgres works with Prisma (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=dc7313e8e0&e=6c74700aa7) , a popular Javascript framework. We're getting close to Doltgres 1.0 (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=f639a486de&e=6c74700aa7) .


** Beads Classic
------------------------------------------------------------

A lot of you are on this mailing list because you are fans of Steve Yegge's Beads (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=91557d9dbb&e=6c74700aa7) , Gas Town (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=e762c23f5b&e=6c74700aa7) , and Wasteland (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=3711fb353f&e=6c74700aa7) . These tools heavily rely on Dolt for task persistence. As part of Beads going 1.0 (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=4b4b0df840&e=6c74700aa7) , Dustin restored embedded Dolt to Beads (https://dolthub.us5.list-manage.com/track/click?u=362cc10f3158476f3baf3a9d6&id=a9c98fb293&e=6c74700aa7) or as Steve likes to call it: Beads Classic.
