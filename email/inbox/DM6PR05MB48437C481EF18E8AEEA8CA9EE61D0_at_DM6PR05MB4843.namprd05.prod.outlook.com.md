Hello Everyone,

We are getting closer to opening up our lovely Radisson Blu Mountain Resort and I am really looking forward to meet everyone in person.

I have attached your welcome letter in English and Norwegian, please read it through.

Under you will find some useful information:

CORONA
You need to keep yourself updated on the latest rules and regulations regarding Covid19 in Norway and regarding your arrival in Norway. Please visit FHI for more and updated information: Link for English: https://www.fhi.no/en/id/infectious-diseases/coronavirus/  Link for Norwegian: https://www.fhi.no/sv/smittsomme-sykdommer/corona/

As for now you need to be in quarantine if you come from abroad, for 10 days, you need to do a Covid-19 test your first day of arrival in Norway, as for now at Trysil Legesenter or on Gardemoen airport. If your test is negative you can start to work, but you still need to be in quarantine on your spare time. This means that you are not allowed to be out in the slopes or hang out with new friends during this time. It is very important that everyone respect these rules. Radisson will cover the costs for your Covid-19 test. (test results normally take 2-3 days to get back, if you do not have a D-number it takes much more time than that) Radisson Trysil are looking for other options.
I will come back to you during next week with more information around Corona from us at Radisson Trysil.

REMEMBER TO BRING:

1.       Black working shoes (sneakers are ok as long as they are completely black). We recommend to bring your own shoes, since you're not guaranteed to purchase the ones you want here.

2.       Black trousers to wear at work. If working in the bar, black jeans is fine, if working in the restaurants wear black trousers (chinos or dress pants).

3.       Bring your passport. You will need it to apply for your Norwegian tax card.

ACCOMONDATION:
I would like everyone who asked for accommodation to let me know what date you will arrive. Don't forget quarantine for 10 days if arriving from abroad. We will organize so that you stay alone for the first ten days when in quarantine if you are not sharing with your partner or close friend, there is no extra cost for that regardless where you stay (only regular monthly payment).

Please don't hesitate to contact me with any questions.

Have a lovely day!
