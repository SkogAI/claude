Welcome to Clerk Skogix
 
As developers, we’ve felt the pain of building auth and user management. Clerk takes care of all of this for you and lets you
focus on what’s important — building your application.
 
It only takes 7 minutes to get up and running, with 3 simple steps to get a complete auth and user management solution:
 
Step 1: Configure your Application
Sign in to the Clerk Dashboard [https://dashboard.clerk.com] and create your first application so you can fully customize the
authentication settings you need for your application.
 
Step 2: Install Clerk Locally
Follow our simple Quickstart Guides [https://clerk.com/docs/quickstarts/overview] and add Clerk’s <SignIn />
[https://clerk.com/docs/components/authentication/sign-in] and <UserProfile />
[https://clerk.com/docs/components/user/user-profile] components into your application.
 
Step 3: Become your first User
Sign up through your newly installed components and start receiving usage analytics immediately!
 
We’re excited to see what you build! If you have any questions or need help getting set up, join our community discord
[https://discord.com/invite/b5rXHjAg7A].
