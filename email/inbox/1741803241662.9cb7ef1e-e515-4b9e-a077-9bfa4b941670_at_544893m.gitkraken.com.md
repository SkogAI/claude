Let’s talk Commit Graph

With features like commit search and robust filtering options, the GitLens Commit Graph lets you quickly locate specific commits, messages, authors, changed files, or even individual code changes. Explore some of the interactive actions below.

Visualization with Purpose

- Insights: View why, who and when lines of code changed with annotations
- Search: Locate commits, branches, and files with search & filtering capabilities
- Interactive: Rebase, merge, and manage commits from the Commit Graph
- Integrations: Link commits to issues, PRs, & Jira tasks
- Comparisons: Compare branches, tags, and commits side by side
