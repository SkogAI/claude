Hey there —
 
It’s been a busy few weeks at Clerk, with updates across analytics, billing, AI tooling, and more. If you haven’t checked the
changelog lately, here’s a quick look at what’s new.
 


New pricing and plans that deliver more value

Clerk’s first major pricing update in years is here and it includes more free retained users, unlimited applications on every
plan, bundled essentials like MFA and simultaneous sessions, and automatic volume discounts.
 



 
Now growing teams get more headroom by default without needing to constantly rethink their pricing tier. Read more →
[https://go.clerk.com/u1lAYtV]




Visualize individual user activity with the new User Activity report

You can now see how often a specific user engages with your product over time via the new User Activity report, right from their
profile page.
 



 
See how often a user is active and catch engagement patterns before they become churn. Read more → [https://go.clerk.com/Vd8XdlX]




Clerk Skills: teach AI agents to help you build with Clerk

Introducing Clerk Skills, installable packages that give AI coding assistants specialized Clerk auth knowledge. Once installed,
your AI agent can help add auth flows, sync users to your database, build custom sign-in screens, and more.
 



 
Spend less time fixing auth flows and more time shipping features with AI tools that understand your auth layer. Read more →
[https://go.clerk.com/VvSUoh5]




Custom billing plans and smooth subscription transitions

Clerk Billing now supports transitioning active subscriptions between plans and prices directly from the Dashboard or API. You can
manage upgrades, downgrades, and promotional pricing without building custom logic.
 



 
No more handling plan migrations manually. Read more → [https://go.clerk.com/RzJL7dD]




Export Dashboard analytics to share insights

Charts in your Clerk Dashboard can now be exported as high-quality images or copied to your clipboard, perfect for sharing trends
with teammates, stakeholders, or on social.
 



 
Share more than just screenshots, get polished metrics in seconds. Read more → [https://go.clerk.com/DexgKQK]




Org Retention Chart

You can now track retention at the organization level, not just individual users. This gives you visibility into how teams engage
with your product over time.
 



 
Understand retention at the account level, not just per user.




Other features, fixes, and improvements

 * iOS & Android SDK v1 [https://go.clerk.com/5MEI5dJ] — Native SDKs, unified auth surface.

 * Require MFA [https://go.clerk.com/NzzZxHA] — Enforce stronger security with one toggle.

 * Enterprise connection testing [https://go.clerk.com/7C2dGrk] — Shareable SSO test links with logs.

 * Improved Stripe account detection [https://go.clerk.com/joTe8le] — Proactive Stripe status warnings.

 
Everything's live and waiting for you.
 
Keep shipping.
