From Monday 11/6 through Thursday 11/9, our team will be working
on the improvements and you may experience minor changes or
temporary interruptions to your Google Drive Applets.

Once complete, here’s what to expect:

* Applets that use Drive’s Create a document action or Append to
a document action will be associated with the new Google Docs
service.
* Applets that use Drive’s Add row to spreadsheet action will be
associated with the new Google Sheets service.
* Applets that use Drive’s Upload file from URL action will
remain associated with the Google Drive service.

No direct action is needed from you right now. If you receive an
email asking you to reconnect the Google Drive service, please do
so.
