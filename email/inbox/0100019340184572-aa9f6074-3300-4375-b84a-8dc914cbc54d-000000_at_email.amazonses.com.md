🌓 Comparison View in Dataset Experiments

 
Datasets are fundamental in LLM application testing. They allow us to iterate and improve on our LLM application based on a list
of test cases (dataset items) and custom evaluation metrics.
 
Langfuse Datasets [https://langfuse.com/docs/datasets/overview] now offers a powerful comparison view for dataset experiment runs.
It's perfect for teams iterating on their AI applications with different prompts, models, or parameters who want to quickly see
the results of a change in a side-by-side with their benchmark version.
 
If you spot an issue in an experiment, you can always jump into the individual trace of the test case, which helps to quickly
reason about the problem and potentially fix it.
 
🔍 What's New?
 * Overviews: See all items of your dataset at a glance.

 * Summaries: Analyze metrics like latency, cost, scores, and the application's output for each experiment side-by-side.
