Top News: OpenAI’s New Image Model Now Available in ComfyUI Workflows
-----------------------------------------------------------------------

( https://www.runpod.io/console/explore/cndsag8ob0 )
OpenAI’s new GPT-Image-1 model, a major leap forward from earlier DALL-E models, is now available through API and fully integrated into ComfyUI, the most widely used open-source workflow tool for AI image and video generation.

This update lets users seamlessly swap older models like FLUX for OpenAI’s best-in-class generator - known for incredible style transfer, text accuracy, and consistency - directly into their ComfyUI pipelines.

Without ComfyUI support, users may have migrated away from community workflows toward closed platforms. Instead, ComfyUI remains the gold standard for flexible, node-based AI generation - and RunPod remains the easiest place to deploy it.
