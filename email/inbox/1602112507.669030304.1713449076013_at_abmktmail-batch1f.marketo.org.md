Password tips from the pros
Use a unique password for every account. Defend yourself against hackers who try to break in using the same username and password combination.
Make passwords at least 12 characters long. Include letters, numbers, and special characters to make each password complex and unguessable.
Avoid commonly used passwords like asd123, password1, or Temp!. Instead, generate totally random strong passwords, like: S&2x4S12nLS1*, JANa@sx3l2&s$, 49915w5$oYmH.
