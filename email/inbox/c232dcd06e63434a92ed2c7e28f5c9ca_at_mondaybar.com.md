MONDAY BAR: THE GRAND 2025 CRUISE, MAY 2-4 • 377 amazing pictures by Timo Torvikoski • Both on Facebook and Flickr (new!) • Checkout the time lapse video by Lucas from Paris • Official after-movie in a few weeks Dear Monday Bar family, Our plan was one big cruise every year. But then we got the opportunity to organise a cruise on M/S Birka Gotland, one of the finest, but not biggest cruise liners on the Baltic Sea. She was decommissioned in 2020 at the beginning of covid, and was held in port at Mariehamn until Gotlandsbolaget and Viking Line decided to take her over as a joint venture last year. And after two successful cruises with Viking Cinderella we have been welcomed back. M/S Birka Gotland is a pure cruise ship with no car deck, and her regular route is Stockholm-Mariehamn, but can also go to other destinations, such as Visby, Gotland, especially during summer. But for us, we will this time make a shorter 21hr trip to Mariehamn, Åland. Many of our guests have requested that we also do shorter, one-day cruises, like parents who can have a hard time to be away longer. So we went on a cruise to see if there had been any changes since we last saw her, more than 5 years ago. And to our great surprise the ship is now even better after millions have been spent on renovation. Do you remember the spectacular car deck floor on Cinderella? Well, Birka Gotland has its own unique floor, the terrace with a detachable glass roof, perfect for dancing under the stars.We have just put up 377 new images from our last cruise by Timo Torvikoski. Both on Facebook, but we have also registered a new account at Flickr, so that Timo’s pictures can be seen in better quality, and as a slide show. The official after-movie is also in the making and should be ready soon. Also, check out this cool time-lapse video <https://www.facebook.com/lucas.lemoine.754/videos/1336914040900055/?idorvanity=102905006416717> made by one of our international guests, Lucas Lemoine from Paris with his own music (Paul van Dyk republished it on TikTok).
