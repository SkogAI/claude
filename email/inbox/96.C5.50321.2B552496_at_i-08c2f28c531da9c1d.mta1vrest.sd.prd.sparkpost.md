 Today, we're excited to launch Replit Learn - our destination for learning 
vibe coding.

Through video lessons, interactive exercises, and hands-on examples, you'll 
discover how to turn your ideas into real applications using AI.

 <https://learn.replit.com/>

 Replit Learn is completely free. No sign-up required for your first lessons. 

Start Today  <https://learn.replit.com/>

The platform focuses on outcomes, not syntax. You learn to communicate with 
AI, shape your vision, and create working software through the
describe-critique-iterateapproach.

 Our first course, AI Foundations, is launching now. The first three lessons 
are available today, with three more coming soon.

Here's a sneak peek:


 * How apps work: Software is all around us — from the alarm clock that wakes 
you up to the browser. You'll learn when building your own app makes sense 
versus using existing tools.
 * How AI works: The AI tools you use today are powered by Large Language 
Models (LLMs). These models predict the next word based on patterns they've 
learned. You'll understand concepts like tokens, non-determinism, and the 
difference between reactive LLMs and agents.
 * How to vibe code: Vibe coding is a new way of creating software. You 
describe what you want in plain language, then work with Replit Agent until 
you've built what you're looking for. You'll learn the basics of vibe coding 
and how to get started.






AI Foundations is just the beginning. We're developing three additional 
courses:


 * Intro to Replit: Get hands-on with the platform, learn to navigate the 
workspace, and deploy your first app
 * Advanced Vibes: Master advanced prompting techniques and build more complex 
applications
 * Replit at Work: Apply vibe coding to professional contexts with internal 
tools and workflow automation

We'll be sharing these as they launch, we hope you'll follow along
