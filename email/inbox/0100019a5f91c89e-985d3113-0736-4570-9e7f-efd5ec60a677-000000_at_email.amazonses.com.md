Score Analytics

Scores [https://langfuse.com/docs/evaluation/overview#core-concepts] track application quality, but getting reliable scores at
scale is difficult. You must know if your evaluation methods are aligned. Score Analytics helps you compare and validate all your
evaluators, from human ratings to LLM-as-a-judge.
Select a score to see its distribution and trend over time. Add a second score to compare two evaluation methods. Correlation
heatmaps and Pearson metrics reveal alignment between evaluators, such as two different LLM judges. This analysis also works for
categorical scores to find relationships between trace properties.
→ Try Score Analytics [https://langfuse.com/changelog/2025-11-07-score-analytics-multi-score-comparison]

LLM-as-a-Judge Tracing

Langfuse now also traces the model calls of your LLM-as-a-Judge
[https://langfuse.com/docs/evaluation/evaluation-methods/llm-as-a-judge] evaluators. This helps you to optimize your evaluators by
tracking their costs over time.
