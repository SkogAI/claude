**🌊 Introducing Wave 10**
-------------------------


 

We’ve officially hit double digits. Wave 10 brings some of the biggest upgrades to how you think, build, and plan inside Windsurf.

 

This launch marks two key steps forward in expanding flow awareness and AI collaboration across the full software development lifecycle, along with a number of other features:
 


- **Planning Mode**: Collaborate with AI on long-term goals. 
- **Windsurf Browser**: A browser built for developer workflows. 
- **Enterprise + UX Upgrades**: More polish, more power, more places to deploy. 
 

Let’s break it down.


**Planning Mode**
-----------------


Planning Mode is a new way to work with AI on long-term engineering tasks. Planning Mode creates and evolves a persistent markdown plan file — one that both you and the Cascade can edit in real time.
 


- Generate a plan based on your goal 
- Cascade uses the plan to drive actions 
- You or Cascade can modify it anytime 
- Plans are updated as the AI learns new info
  
This gives you a real-time, editable long-term plan shared between you and Cascade. It’s available now on all **paid tiers at no extra cost**. [[Read the full blog post here.](https://windsurf.com/blog/windsurf-wave-10-planning-mode)]


**Windsurf Browser**
--------------------


Your browser is now part of your shared timeline.

 

The Windsurf Browser is a fully functional, AI-integrated browser. Cascade knows what tabs you have open and what content you're looking at – no copy-pasting, no sharing URLs. Just ask, and Cascade can use the context of what you’re already working on.

 

For the first time, your browser joins your terminal, editor, and conversations as part of the full developer surface Windsurf understands. The Windsurf Browser is in beta for all self-serve plans. [[Read the full blog post here](https://windsurf.com/blog/windsurf-wave-10-browser).]


**Other Updates**
-----------------


- Europe Cluster: A new Frankfurt-based datacenter for EU customers. Fully operational from day one, with local inference and data retention.
- Cascade in metric now tracks code edits from Cascade too – not just Tab.

 

**UX Improvements**
-------------------

- Browse past conversations without losing your current one
- File-type icons for easier Cascade citation scanning
- Interact with terminal commands inside Cascade
- Code blocks now follow your Windsurf theme
- Sunset of Legacy Mode – everyone’s now using Write & Chat

 

You can read about all the UX improvements we’ve introduced in the blog post [here](https://windsurf.com/blog/windsurf-wave-10-ux-enterprise).


**What's Next**
---------------


Wave 10 extends flow awareness into your long-term plans and your web activity. It sets the stage for deeper collaboration between you and Cascade – not just on what you’re building, but how and why you’re building it.

 

We’ll keep pushing the limits. And as always, we’re listening.

 

**Surf’s up.**
