Hi Emil,
 
January was a big month. New design, MCP support, increased recording retention, and more. Here's what's new.
 


New design

We've refreshed the look and feel of Circleback [https://circleback.ai/releases/new-design]. Every pixel has been
reconsidered–updated colors, a new typeface, and a simpler interface that makes it easier to find what you need and get your work
done.



 


Circleback MCP

You can now connect AI tools to Circleback using MCP [https://circleback.ai/releases/circleback-mcp]. Search and access context
from your meetings, calendar, emails, and more in AI apps like ChatGPT, Claude, and Cursor.



Here's [https://x.com/iAligator/status/2011130784565117134] how I used it to update our onboarding by just mentioning a meeting–no
other context needed.
 


Manage tags

You can now rename tags [https://circleback.ai/releases/manage-tags], change their color, add a description for automatic meeting
tagging, and delete tags you no longer need from Settings → Tags.



 


Other releases

 * Faster filtering with your keyboard [https://circleback.ai/releases/faster-filtering-with-your-keyboard]

 * Recording retention increased to 2 years [https://circleback.ai/releases/recording-retention-increased-to-2-years]

 
We also released major improvements to the desktop recording experience on macOS, with similar updates for Windows coming soon.
