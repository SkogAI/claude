From hardcoded credentials to overprivileged service accounts and missed offboarding, attackers are finding new ways to exploit the landscape of machine identities.

 

But what does a modern approach to NHI and secrets governance look like?

 

On May, 6 join GitGuardian, CyberArk, and CNCF experts for a practical, story-driven session on managing non-human identities (NHIs) and secrets.

 

We’ll dive into:

* Common risks: unmanaged NHIs, overprivileged accounts, and long-lived secrets
* Moving toward ephemeral secrets and “secretless” architectures
* How to detect and respond when NHI secrets are leaked or abused—with a live look at Falco threat detection
* Real-world incidents including the GTA VI breach, with lessons for your organization
Expect actionable insights, expert tips, and live Q&A.
