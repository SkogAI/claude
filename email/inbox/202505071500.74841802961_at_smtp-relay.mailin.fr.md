Hello Emil, 

 

We wanted to take a moment to share the story behind Recall.

Our co-founder and CEO, Paul, comes from the cybersecurity industry, where he used knowledge graphs to connect fragmented intelligence and uncover hidden patterns. Outside of work, he faced a similar challenge—he was consuming tons of content but struggling to recall insights when it mattered.

 

That’s when it clicked: he needed a knowledge graph for everything he was learning.

**The Foundations of Recall**

Recall organizes your content in a knowledge graph, automatically linking related information. With our newest feature, **Augmented Browsing**, these connections resurface as you browse—revealing insights you might have missed.

[ Learn More](https://docs.getrecall.ai/deep-dives/recall-augmented-browsing)

Check it out—we hope it resonates with you as much as it does with us. And please do share your candid feedback on how we can improve.
