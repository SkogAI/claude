📱 Meet the Modern SSH Client on Your iPhone

Earlier this month, Termius for iOS got a fresh new look, but it's more than just a new appearance. We've reimagined the entire terminal experience on mobile, enhancing your interactions by taking advantage of the touchscreen’s capabilities and making troubleshooting on mobile devices easier. And as always, we aim to keep you productive even when you’re away from your laptop.

*🫵 Gestures for Arrow Keys*

The touchscreen on the iPhone is very powerful. It allows you to accomplish so many things with just a few swipes. In Termius, we bring gestures to a whole new level. Now, you can simulate the arrow keys with a long press and drag gesture. This new approach allows you to use both scroll and gestures without switching the mode, as it used to be.

*⌨️ Streamlined Quick-Access Keyboard*

Interacting with the terminal can be tricky without a physical keyboard. That's why we've rethought our quick-access keyboard for the mobile terminal. And don't worry; you can always customize the quick-access keys to match your preferences. Tap the extended keyboard button to see all the predefined key groups, then hit “Edit” to adjust them.

*🧠 AI Assist at Your Fingertips*

AI is now the number one action on your keyboard. Let's be honest: remembering all the commands, arguments, and parameters is challenging, as is typing them on a tiny mobile screen. That's why we've pinned the AI "Magic Button" to your keyboard. Type what you want to get the correct command with AI, or even dictate it using speech-to-text 🎙️

*✳️ Context-Aware Password Paste*

Pasting passwords in the terminal is now easier. Autocomplete shows your password suggestions whenever a password is prompted. To see them, enable autocomplete in Settings -> Autocomplete and get password suggestions only when needed.

*🔠 Easier Access to Snippets & Commands*

The new quick-access keyboard only shows essential keys for a desktop-like terminal experience. Yet, Snippets and Command History are still just one tap away. Tap the extended keyboard button to find them, just like accessing emojis in messages. This additional keyboard remembers your last selected tab, so tapping the extended keyboard button again reopens that tab.

The Snippets and Command History tabs have also been updated. Instead of pasting a complex command to the terminal to see what it does, you can preview it with a long press—fewer actions for better productivity.

*🏝️ Live Activity for your Port Forwarding Rules*

The Live Activity feature has been extended. Now, your active port forwarding connections and terminal sessions will appear on your iPhone’s Dynamic Island and Lock Screen. This allows you to monitor your connections in the background. You can quickly open the Port Forwarding screen by tapping the active rule displayed there.

🌏 Shared Vaults Are Everywhere

Great news for teams using Termius on desktop and mobile! Multiple vaults are now fully supported on Android. This means you can create as many vaults as needed with granular permissions. And your team can access them on Windows ( https://termius.com/download/windows ) , Linux ( https://termius.com/download/linux ) , macOS ( https://termius.com/download/macos ) , iOS ( https://termius.com/download/ios ) , and now Android ( https://termius.com/download/android ).

If you haven't tried Termius mobile, now is the perfect time. With access to all your team's vaults, your teammates can respond to incidents even when away from their keyboards. This added flexibility ensures your team can stay productive and responsive, no matter where they are.
