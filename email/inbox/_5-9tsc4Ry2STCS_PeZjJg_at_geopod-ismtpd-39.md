🗄️ Vaults: Manage Team Access Easily

Every team in Termius has a Team Vault—encrypted cloud storage shared with the entire team. Everything you move into the Team vault is instantly available to all team members across all their devices. But what if you need more granular access control?

*More Vaults, More Control*
Create additional vaults to share different data with different teammates. This way, only the right people have access. Each vault can have its own access permissions:

* *Editors* : Can create, update, and remove data.
* *Viewers* : Can only connect and use snippets.

*Share a Single Host*
Sometimes, you need to share a single host with just one person, like a contractor. While you can't share a single host directly, you can create a new vault just for that. Move the host there, add a member to the vault, and remove it when done. It keeps things secure and simple.

*Why Use Vaults*

* *Security:* End-to-end encryption keeps your data safe.
* *Control:* Only intended people have access.
* *Flexibility:* Permissions can be customized for each vault.
* *Efficiency:* Team members access only what they need to stay productive and nothing more.

*Want more control?* Upgrade to Business ( https://termius.com/pricing ) to create additional vaults.

✨ AIOps with AI Agent

What if you could run your entire infrastructure using just a chat interface, like ChatGPT? Just share your intended outcome and watch AI do most of the work for you. At Termius, we’ve been exploring AIOps — a new approach to remote infrastructure maintenance using the latest innovations in LLMs. Meet the AI Agent in Termius.

The AI Agent works similarly to ChatGPT or Claude, but with deep integration into Termius for enhanced productivity. Tell it what you need, and it handles the execution on its own. The AI Agent knows all the required host information, manages connections, runs commands, and processes their output. This enables a revolutionary new workflow that completely transforms how DevOps teams work.

This is our first step toward radically transforming DevOps workflows with AI to boost productivity. Our initial version successfully handles common diagnostic tasks like opening ports, managing system updates, and installing software. While more complex tasks still need refinement, we’re continuously improving the system’s capabilities.

We understand that using AI comes with important security and privacy considerations. That’s why we’re launching a limited private beta for a small number of early adopters. This will allow us to enhance the user experience and implement additional security measures before the public release.
