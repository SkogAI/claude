*****************************************************************************************************
VACE: Turn Photos Into Hollywood-Level Video ( https://blog.runpod.io/how-to-deploy-vace-on-runpod/ )
*****************************************************************************************************

Imagine taking a single photo and bringing it to life with realistic motion — or seamlessly expanding a vertical phone video to cinematic widescreen format while intelligently filling in the missing background.

This isn't science fiction. It's VACE (Video All-in-One Creation and Editing) ( https://blog.runpod.io/how-to-deploy-vace-on-runpod/ ), Alibaba's groundbreaking open-source AI model that's revolutionizing video content creation. Instead of juggling multiple specialized tools, VACE lets you generate videos from text prompts, animate still images, swap characters, and control object motion — all in a single unified platform.

***************
Why it matters:
***************

*
Move-Anything capabilities let you precisely control motion trajectories of any object in your video
* Swap-Anything functionality replaces characters, objects, or elements with reference images seamlessly
* Reference-Anything technology uses any image as style or content reference to transform your video
*
Animate-Anything features bring still photos to life with realistic motion and cinematic quality

*******************
How to get started:
*******************

*
Choose "Pods" from the left menu
* Select your preferred GPU configuration
* Search for "VACE" in "Pod Template" under "Configure Deployment"
* Click "Deploy On-Demand" at the bottom
Deploy VACE on a Pod ( https://www.runpod.io/console/deploy?template=758dsjwiqz&type=gpu )

AlphaEvolve ( https://deepmind.google/discover/blog/alphaevolve-a-gemini-powered-coding-agent-for-designing-advanced-algorithms/ )
----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DeepMind introduced AlphaEvolve - a Gemini-driven research agent that automatically designs improved algorithms. Instead of relying on human-crafted heuristics, AlphaEvolve evolves entirely new code for core algorithms like matrix multiplication. Early results are already improving compute efficiency inside Google's data centers.

***************
