MiniMax M2.5 and Z AI GLM-5 models

https://ollama.com/library/minimax-m2.5

 
MiniMax M2.5 is an open-source frontier coding and agentic model strike a good balance between quality and speed.
 
MiniMax and Ollama partnered to bring M2.5 on Ollama's cloud for free for the next few days.
 
ollama launch --model minimax-m2.5:cloud
 
Learn more about MiniMax M2.5 on Ollama [https://ollama.com/library/minimax-m2.5].
 

https://ollama.com/library/glm-5

Z AI's GLM-5 model is also on Ollama's cloud. It is positioned as a general purpose open-source frontier model that is good across
reasoning, creative writing, coding, and agentic tasks.
 
ollama launch --model glm-5:cloud
 
Learn more about GLM-5 on Ollama [https://ollama.com/library/glm-5].
