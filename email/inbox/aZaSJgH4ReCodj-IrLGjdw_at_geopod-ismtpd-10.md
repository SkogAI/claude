Hopefully by now, your website is published and you're ready to grow. Here are some quick tips:

* If you haven’t yet, connect Google Analytics ( https://support.soloist.ai/doc/how-do-i-view-analytics-for-my-website/ ) to see how much traffic your website is receiving.
* If you have set up a custom domain ( https://support.soloist.ai/doc/connect-custom-domain-to-solo-free/ ) for your website, connect Google Search Console ( https://support.soloist.ai/doc/how-do-i-connect-google-search-console/ ) and see how Google search ranks your website.
* To improve your Google rank, add your new website to your social pages such as Yelp, Facebook, Instagram and more.
* Create a Google Business and an Apple Business Profile ( https://support.soloist.ai/doc/how-to-set-up-a-google-business-and-apple-business-profile/ ) to appear in search results on Google and Apple maps.
