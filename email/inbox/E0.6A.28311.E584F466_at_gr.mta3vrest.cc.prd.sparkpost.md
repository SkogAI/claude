Let's tell it like it is. No one
scrolls Kivra for fun. But life is life; there are dinners to
cook, documents to handle, and bills to pay. That's why we're
here, gathering all the must-dos in one
place.

*****************************************************************
Food – now in
Kivra

*****************************************************************

Well, not literally, but now
you can keep track of great food prices in the app. If you want,
we'll give you a shout when there's a particularly nicely priced
cheese in your area. Coop is first out with offers in Kivra, and
more stores are on their way. The concept is pretty simple: a
helping hand for your weekly shopping. And fewer flyers on your
doormat. Better for
everyone!

*****************************************************************
A brief story about a thousand
papers

*****************************************************************

Imagine a thousand A4. In your
home. All piled up. Does the thought of that make you happy? If
not, maybe this will: you no longer have to deal with that kind
of stuff. Now, you can upload one thousand documents to Kivra.
Perfect for all those papers you somehow need but not today or
tomorrow; you just need to keep them. Let us keep them for you.
Also, we've added a neat scanning feature that makes uploading
simpler for everyone.

*****************************************************************
The boring stuff should be
easy

*****************************************************************

To simplify the dull things,
we've improved our payment service. Fewer BankID signatures
(finally). Pay multiple bills in one go. Or snap a photo of your
paper bill and pay it in Kivra, even if it came in the snail
mail. And eventually, we'll launch an administrative dream:
direct debit, but in Kivra. Our goal, as always, is for you to
spend as little time as possible in our app. Because there's more
to life than the mundane.
