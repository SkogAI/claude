Discover more with Perplexity

You’re just getting started with Perplexity, and there’s so much more to explore. Let’s look at a few features that will transform
your search experience.




Library: Your personal knowledge hub

Every search you make is automatically saved in your Library, so your personal knowledge base builds over time. Take it a step
further by organizing your search Threads into Collections. Keep them private or share them with others—it's up to you.




Pro Search: When you need to go deeper

When a question demands more, activate Pro Search. It expands our search to three times more sources, giving you richer, more
comprehensive results. As a free user, you get 5 Pro Searches every four hours. Need more? Perplexity Pro
[https://www.perplexity.ai/hub/faq/what-is-perplexity-pro] offers hundreds daily.
 



 


AI Profile: Tailored to you

We all consume information differently. Set up your profile
[https://www.perplexity.ai/settings/profile?utm_source=onboardingemail2] to receive answers that match your style—whether you
prefer concise summaries or in-depth analysis. You can even prioritize your trusted sources.

https://www.perplexity.ai/settings/profile?utm_source=onboardingemail2


File Upload: Turn your files into insights

Need to analyze a complex report or dataset? Simply drag and drop it into Perplexity. Extract specific information, get summaries,
fact-check, or find related sources online. Your files become a springboard for deeper exploration.
