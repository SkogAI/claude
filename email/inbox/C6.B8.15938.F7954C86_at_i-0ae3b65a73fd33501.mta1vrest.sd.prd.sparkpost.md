Build more with MCP: now included in Zapier tasks 
Since launching Zapier MCP back in April, we've been blown away by the 
response. The way our community has jumped in by experimenting with AI tools 
and building assistants right inside their workflows has been inspiring to see.

And today, we're excited to share an important update: Starting September 18, 
MCP tool calls will now draw from your existing Zapier task quota. 
What's changing? 

 * Unified billing: MCP tool calls will now draw down from your existing 
Zapier task quota. 
 * Simple ratio: Each MCP call will count as two Zapier tasks. 
 * No extra limits: Scale MCP usage by upgrading your Zapier plan with no 
separate quota to track. 
What this means for you: 

 * The existing free 300 MCP call quota will end on September 18, 2025. 
 * Any MCP usage after September 18 will count toward your Zapier task quota. 
 * You'll see MCP usage displayed alongside your task usage on mcp.zapier.com. 
This update makes it simple to grow with MCP, as it's now just part of the 
Zapier experience you already know. So whether you're boosting productivity 
with tools like Claude or ChatGPT, or building AI-powered apps for your own 
users, you can scale with confidence as your needs evolve.
