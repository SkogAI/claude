Connect directly to Apple and Google billing systems through Bubble







Hi there,



One of our most highly anticipated features is here! In-app purchases for 
mobile subscriptions is now in beta.


So, if you've been waiting to monetize your mobile app through subscriptions 
you can now connect directly to Apple App Store and Google Play billing systems 
right from Bubble.



Curious about how it works?



 Set up subscription billing using the same workflows and data logic you 
already know. Bubble guides you through connecting to Apple and Google, handles 
receipt validation, and automatically tracks subscription status — so you can 
focus on building instead of managing billing.



 This beta supports subscriptions (like monthly, annual, etc.) so you can sell 
digital content, premium features, or recurring services.



Ready to get started?


 * Head to the Native mobile settings tab
 *  Check out the Bubble Manual 
<https://manual.bubble.io/help-guides/getting-started/building-for.../native-ios-and-android/in-app-purchases>
 for setup steps or watch thewalkthrough tutorial 
<https://app.arcade.software/share/MljxkeAxM25axsb7dCv3>. 

We can't wait to see your first subscribers roll in!



Quick heads up: We'll be gathering feedback and making improvements during the 
beta. When it ends, we plan to implement a 1% fee on subscription revenue above 
$5,000 per month (calculated on gross revenue before app store fees). We don't 
have a timeline yet, but wanted you to know so you can plan accordingly.
