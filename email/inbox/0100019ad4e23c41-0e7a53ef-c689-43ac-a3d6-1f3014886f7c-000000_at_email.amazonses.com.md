The user onboarding flow plays a crucial role in your application development journey, and Clerk simplifies this process for you.
 
We put together a short walkthrough to help you get set up where you will learn to:
 
 1. Add custom claims to your session token

 1. Configure your middleware to read session data

 1. Update your application state based on the user’s onboarding state
