January was a big month for us. We grew revenue 50%, welcomed our first engineer to the team, and completely rebuilt the product
from scratch. Here's what's been going on and where we're headed.
 
The Big News: Riptide Is Almost Ready
 
We're putting the finishing touches on our complete product rebuild and getting ready to ship Riptide to new users. If you've been
on the waitlist, we're almost there.
 
Why We Rebuilt
 
Late last year, we realized something important. We could help one great engineer become an AI-first engineer in a single day. But
when they turned around and gave the same tools to their team, results varied wildly. Even with solid tooling, there's a ton of
nuance and subtlety to getting AI coding right.
 



 
 
The original thesis was that everyone would figure out the "ship 2-3x faster" part eventually, and our differentiation would be in
collaboration. Turns out there was way more room for differentiation in the core workflow than we thought.
 
So we did what any reasonable team would do: we threw out the old codebase and built something new from scratch. The result is a
fully cloud-native, collaborative, and highly-performant system that sets the stage for teams to completely rewire how they build
software.
 
Product Updates
 



 
 * Complete rebuild - Cloud-native, collaboration-first architecture with state-of-the-art workflows

 * New workflow system - Research → Design → Structure → Plan → Implement, with each phase producing shareable artifacts

 * Deep Linear integration - Paste a ticket ID, get a folder, task, and worktree. Docs auto-attach to tickets with smart labels.
   Tickets advance as Claude works.

 * Session auto-labeling and summaries - Know what happened in every session

 * Guided workflow steps - The tool prompts you to move to the next step when ready
