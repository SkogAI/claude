CRUISE No 60 - THE LAST MONDAY BAR CRUISE In our Facebook group, a few weeks ago, we wrote that our upcoming cruise in October might be the last one, and that we will make a decision shortly. Well, now we have, and yes, Monday Bar Cruise No 60 Jubilee will indeed be our last. There are several reasons for this, but mainly that Johnny and Staffan, the owners of the Monday Bar concept have both reached 60 years of age with certain health issues, and are finding it increasingly difficult to find inspiration and energy like we’ve had in the past. We’ve always known that all good things eventually have to come to an end, and so must the cruises at one point. So why not do it with a bang while we’re still on top of our game, with one final cruise! With two months to go, more than 50% of all cabins are already booked, but we expect that after this announcement tickets will sell out quicker than usual. So don’t wait too long to book tickets for you preferred cabin type. We’ll, here we are, and it’s time again to board the best party ship of the Tallink-Silja fleet, M/S Baltic Queen. The cruise is in the beginning of October, but thanks to an outside sun deck partly covered with a roof and wind shield we should be able to enjoy a daytime party on deck while in port in Tallinn on Saturday.
