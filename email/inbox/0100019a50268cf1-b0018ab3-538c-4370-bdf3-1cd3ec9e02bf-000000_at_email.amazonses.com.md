Hi Emil Skogsund,
We’re rolling on with Launch Week 4, and Day 2 is all about connecting your team and your tools. We're launching comments to make
collaboration faster within Langfuse and a new Mixpanel integration to combine LLM insights with your core product analytics.

https://youtu.be/h5mgrtf3xzE

 


@Mentions and Reactions in Comments

Collaborate with your team directly in Langfuse. You can now tag teammates with @mentions to instantly bring their attention to
specific sessions, traces, or prompts. Use emoji reactions to quickly acknowledge insights and share feedback, all in one place.
→ More on comments [https://langfuse.com/changelog/2025-11-04-comment-mentions-and-reactions]

https://langfuse.com/changelog/2025-11-04-comment-mentions-and-reactions

 


Mixpanel integration

We teamed up with Mixpanel [https://mixpanel.com/] to bring your LLM metrics directly into your existing product analytics
dashboards.
This integration helps you combine product analytics with LLM metrics to answer critical questions, like how LLM engagement
impacts retention or how user feedback in Langfuse correlates with behavior in Mixpanel.
→ View an example dashboard [https://eu.mixpanel.com/public/V92wFA5YwNjKn9K9vcUmAX]
→ Get Started [https://langfuse.com/changelog/2025-11-04-mixpanel-integration]

https://langfuse.com/changelog/2025-11-04-mixpanel-integration

 


Amazon Bedrock AgentCore Integration

We've also launched native support for Amazon Bedrock Agents and Knowledge Bases [https://aws.amazon.com/bedrock/agentcore/]. You
can now use the Langfuse SDKs to automatically trace agents, giving you full visibility into your Bedrock-powered RAG and agent
workflows.
→ Get started [https://langfuse.com/changelog/2025-11-04-amazon-bedrock-agentcore-integration],
 


IdP-Initiated SSO

To improve the login experience, we've added support for IdP-initiated SSO. Users can now start authentication directly from their
identity provider's dashboard (like Okta, Azure AD, or Keycloak) for seamless access to Langfuse.
→ Learn more [https://langfuse.com/changelog/2025-11-04-idp-initiated-sso]
 


All releases of Launch Week 4

Day 1: [https://langfuse.com/blog/2025-10-29-launch-week-4] We launched New Filters for Tables and an enhanced API to query traces
and observations.
Day 2: [https://langfuse.com/blog/2025-10-29-launch-week-4] We added Team Collaboration features (like @mentions) and a new
Mixpanel Integration.
