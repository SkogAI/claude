🔄 Update Passwords on Multiple Hosts at Once

Password rotation can be painful when you need to update every single host one by one. But not anymore! With identities and groups, you can change the password in one place to update all hosts at once.

Here's how:

* *Create an Identity* in the *Keychain* with a username and password.
* *Create a Group* and move the relevant hosts there.
* *Attach the Identity* to the Group; all the hosts inside will inherit it.

Next time you have to rotate a password, update it in that one identity, and you're done!
