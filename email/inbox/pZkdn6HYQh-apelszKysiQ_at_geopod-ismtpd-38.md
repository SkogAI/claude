📄 Logs Reinvented
-----------------

Knowing what changed last on a server can be the key to resolving an outage, yet it’s often hard to find out in a team environment. The new approach to Logs allows you to see the latest terminal session on any host, regardless of who connected.

You no longer need to track down the last person connected to find out what they did. Go to the Logs tab, find the most recent log of a host, and start troubleshooting right away.

In the first version available in Beta ( https://termius.com/beta-program ) , we focused on providing access to the most recent change on a host, a simple yet common use case for logs. This allowed us to handle all the technical heavy lifting, ensuring logs are secure and ready for syncing and sharing through your vaults.

Now, we’re looking for teams with broader use cases—those who want to store more logs for longer periods. If you’d like to log all your sessions without limits and are open to discussing your use cases and workflow, get in touch ( https://termius.typeform.com/to/E9XVyoIb#email=emil.skogsund@gmail.com ).
