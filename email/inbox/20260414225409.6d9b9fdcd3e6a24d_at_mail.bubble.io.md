 <https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLoNVJrdvQQYnspOK4NkUSkzZkRYfrVQle>



Is it possible to build a business that does well financially AND makes a real 
difference to people’s lives? 🤔



Giselle Gonzales bet on it and built EqualReach <https://www.equalreach.io/>, 
a freelancing marketplace connecting businesses with refugee talent, which only 
makes money when that talent gets paid.



You’ll hear all about it in our latest episode of The New Build. 

Listen now  <https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=CrQi3Vo8QY8>

Abhinav sat down with the founder, Giselle Gonzales, and Olena Voloshyna, the 
platform's product lead. Olena joined the team when Giselle was accepted into 
Bubble's Immerse program and needed someone to lead the product. A Ukrainian 
refugee herself, Olena had a clear understanding of what the platform actually 
needed to do.



Giselle also gets into how trust shapes the way she hires and builds, and it's 
one of the more universally applicable parts of the conversation for any 
founder growing a team, regardless of what kind of company they're building.



Both guests make the case that the founders who build something lasting tend 
to start with a problem they've lived with.
