When does the plan start?

 

Your payment plan will begin as soon as the store processes your order,
typically within 1-3 days. Until then, your plan will show as "Order processing"
under the Payments tab in your Klarna app.

 

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

 

What should I know about Amazon orders and multiple plans?

 

Amazon may process items separately, so your plan could update over several
days, and you’ll only be charged for items you receive. If you need another plan
while waiting, tap “Options” on your current plan in the Klarna app and select
“Create a new plan.” Your original plan will update automatically once Amazon
finishes processing.
