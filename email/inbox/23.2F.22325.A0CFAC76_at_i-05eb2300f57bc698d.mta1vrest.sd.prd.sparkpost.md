Important Update: Billing for storing logs in AI Gateway begins April 15, 2025 Hi, We wanted to update you on the billing timeline for storing logs in AI Gateway: billing for persistent log storage will begin on April 15, 2025. You will only be charged for log storage beyond the included volume in your Workers plan (see below). Workers Free Workers Paid Included Volume (no cost) 100,000 logs stored (total) 200,000 logs stored (total) Additional Logs N/A $8 per 100,000 logs stored per month Key Details: Workers Free Plan: Store up to 100,000 logs total across gateways at no charge. Workers Paid Plan: Store up to 200,000 logs total across gateways at no charge. Additional logs are available at $8 per 100,000 logs stored per month. Track Usage: You can track the number of logs stored anytime in the AI Gateway Dashboard. Configure Log Storage: To manage your log storage effectively, you can: Set Storage Limits: Configure a limit on the number of logs stored per gateway in your gateway settings to ensure you only pay for what you need. Enable Automatic Log Deletion: Activate the Automatic Log Deletion feature to automatically delete the oldest logs once the log limit you’ve set or the default storage limit of 10 million logs is reached. This ensures new logs are always saved without manual intervention. For more details, refer to our documentation. If you've been using persistent logs but do not wish to incur charges, please ensure your stored logs are within your plan's included volume before April 14th, 2025. Logs exceeding the free limit after this date will be billed according to your plan. For users who prefer to export logs to external storage or 3rd parties, Logpush is also available. For guidance on managing or deleting logs, please see our developer documentation. As a reminder, we highly recommend enabling Authenticated Gateway to prevent unauthorized access. Thank you for using AI Gateway!
