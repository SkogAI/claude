Set up OpenClaw

Open a terminal, and type in:
 
ollama launch openclaw
 
By default, Ollama will also configure openclaw to use its web search functionality.
 
If you want to customize your OpenClaw after to connect different chat apps or other integrations, please run:
 
openclaw onboard
