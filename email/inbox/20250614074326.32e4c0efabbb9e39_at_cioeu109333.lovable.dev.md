Hey, we have some exciting news:

-----------------------------------
Lovable is free to use this weekend
-----------------------------------

Lovable is free for everyone over this weekend, starting now until Sunday 15th of June 23:59 CET!

During this time, credits will not be deducted when making prompts.

There might be rate limits during high demand, where paid users get prioritized access.

Start building for free: lovable.dev ( https://lovable.dev/ )

---------------
The AI Showdown
---------------

The reason Lovable is free this weekend is because we've partnered with OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google to host The AI Showdown: a public comparison of the world’s leading AI models.

During The AI Showdown, for the first time, you will be able to easily switch what AI model to power Lovable.

During the weekend we're hosting two competitions:

* Content challenge ($25,000 prize): Create the best content focused on comparing the capabilities of the models inside Lovable.
* Build challenge ($40,000 prize): A build challenge to showcase what each model can create inside Lovable.

You can participate and follow the live model comparison dashboard at: aishowdown.lovable.app ( https://aishowdown.lovable.app/ )

Learn more about The AI Showdown here ( https://x.com/lovable_dev/status/1933643500727222322 ).
