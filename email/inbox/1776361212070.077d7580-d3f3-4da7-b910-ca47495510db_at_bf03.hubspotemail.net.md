Better at long tasks. Better at following your prompts literally.

NEW MODEL

Opus 4.7 is live in Zenflow and Zencoder

Anthropic's newest model is now available on Core, Advanced, and Max plans. Select it in Zenflow Code, Zenflow Work, or the IDE Plugins and your next task runs on it.

Opus 4.7 is built for the hardest coding work. It handles complex, long-running tasks with rigor, pays precise attention to instructions, and verifies its own outputs before reporting back. The kind of work that used to need close supervision can now be handed off.

WHAT'S NEW IN OPUS

Four changes worth knowing

LONG-HORIZON WORK

Sees hard tasks through to the end

Multi-file refactors, migrations, and feature builds that used to drift halfway through now hold their approach. The model catches its own logical faults during planning and recovers from tool failures instead of restarting.

INSTRUCTION FOLLOWING

Takes your prompts literally

Opus 4.7 follows instructions more precisely than earlier models. That's usually what you want, but prompts tuned for Opus 4.6 can produce unexpected results. Worth re-reading your saved prompts and agent configs.

VISION

Reads dense screenshots and diagrams

Opus 4.7 handles images up to 2,576 pixels on the long edge, more than three times the resolution of earlier Claude models. Useful for pasting in architecture diagrams, Figma exports, or log screenshots without losing detail.

MEMORY

Remembers across sessions

Better at reading and writing file-based notes across long, multi-session work. New tasks need less up-front context because the model picks up where it left off.

HOW TO USE IT

Pick it from the model menu

Create a Zenflow task, select Opus 4.7 from the Custom preset picker, and you're on it.

Available on Core, Advanced, and Max. Works across Zenflow Code, Zenflow Work, and the IDE plugins.
