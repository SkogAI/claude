💡 Tip of the Month 💡

One question that has come up often is about how Daily Active Users (https://djX2BV04.eu1.hubspotlinks.com/Ctc/5H+113/djX2BV04/MVZZypn6yb9W1sjkVh4yW3nJW1FPBGm5sPjsWN9k7M8K5n4LbW50kH_H6lZ3nRW5xmF5j6Px5qtW7jZCJc382xdYN4XGSF8Hzjh6W6k9pZd2D0H1FW4V4Dvd7v2ZMTV1R1-Y2NM-1QW2lT-gp1HWxXjW4cnMQY3Hh_NCVbZbFr40C8l9W79006F71hCWxW3F9fdH4mR5cZW2J8TxY99jH1_W5pZX5_47WKFBW5t_NtN72FTlDW9005473yPzLBW2jNBYS2gTBDLW34LBdg2bPWWLW109Zn93HprxSW77HNLR5Q6ch_N4dyTQMT4V8FW8R5-NX591s-DW6pnWc31w4Dq_W7k4SzZ6kZK4qW7mZd353sx74vW2yMZWB7DjmZfW4mn4b-2LX_bNW19LdGD8VJYkwN7FlcbdKzfblW3p4LWG7tYT4FW37YWpL5hZCD0N4jj_KXNQgv-W4kc93D8J6Qxmf8g62HW04 ) are calculated:

At Zitadel we assess the number of daily active users by day, and then use the sum total over the course of the month. For example, if you have 1 user who authenticates every day across a given month (30 days), that sums up to 30 daily active users. If you have 5 users where each user authenticates 1 time per week over the course of 4 weeks, this would calculate as 20 daily active users.
