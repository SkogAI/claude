Over the past year, we’ve seen a surge in large-scale agentic workloads — involving millions of namespaces, small namespace sizes, and sporadic, bursty query patterns. In response, we’ve evolved our architecture with innovations across both our query and write paths, including:

- Adaptive indexing using log-structured merge trees
- Support for millions of namespaces per index—without sacrificing performance or cost

We’ve also boosted search and recommendation performance with enhanced metadata filtering, high-performance sparse indexing for keyword search, automatic replication at high QPS (provisioned capacity options coming soon), and more.

Starting this week, new users on Pinecone will run on our most advanced

architecture yet. We will update the community as we roll this out to everyone.
