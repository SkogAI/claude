*🔀 Smart Merge: Combine Model Responses Seamlessly*

And to top it all with *Smart Merge* you can integrate the responses from multiple models into a single one, say goodbye to analysis paralysis! Get the best out of AI in one single place.

Choose from three Merge Options functionalities:

* *Unify Responses* : Combine all selected outputs into a single response.
* *Highlight Differences* : Focus on variances between models' responses.
* *Custom Prompts* : Craft tailored instructions for the final merged response.
