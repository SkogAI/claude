Limited number of tickets • Online tickets + at C.U.M. • Extra big checkin for bags, etc Dear Monday Bar Family, The cruise is now only one week away. In this newsletter we will provide all information you need for the cruise and afterparty. A FEW LAST CABINS LEFT WERE JUST RELEASED With an almost much full ship with 99,5% of all cabins are now booked. 4 more cabins (mostly 2 person inside cabins) were just released. Also, if you have a standard cabin booked for only 1-3 persons, you can still upgrade to a maximum of 4 persons. GET ONBOARD FROM 16.25 - THE PARTY STARTS DIRECTLY AT BACKSTAGE, DECK 5 You can go onboard from 16.25. Latest boarding: 17.10. The cruise leaves 17.30 sharp. But the party already starts 16.30 at Backstage floor, deck 5 PARKING CLOSE TO THE FERRY TERMINAL There’s a car park next to the ferry terminal. But make sure you are in good time since the traffic in Stockholm can be congested Friday afternoon. REQUIRED IDENTIFICATION (Don’t forget!) Nordic citizens: Valid ID or Driver License (or passport) Non-Nordic citizens: Passport or National ID card AFTER PARTY TICKETS A limited number of online tickets for The Cruise Afterparty, Nov 8, directly after the cruise are now available. The remaining tickets will be sold in C.U.M’s shop on the cruise. If you are worried that there won’t be any left we can reserve tickets to be picked up between 23.00-00.00. Please send an email with your name and how many to: staffan@mondaybar.com Birka Gotland loves Monday Bar! The ship has again asked us to remind everyone to book dinner. Since the cruise will be completely sold out, we strongly advise you to book in advance, and they offer a very good pre-booking discount for all buffet bookings. Here’s a message from the ship: Don't miss out on pre-booking the Buffet including drinks for the fantastic price of 350 SEK/person, on board it costs 480-525 SEK/person. Call Viking Line on 08-4524000 to book the Buffet. Or login online to your booking. Pre-order a drink package for your cabin pre-party Since alcohol is not offered at the Duty-free shop on Friday night (only on Saturday), they have an offer to pre-order a pack of either 4 Heinekens or 4 Long Drinks that can be picked up at the conference desk (deck 6). Prices are 65 SEK per bottle/can (chilled), but you will be able to avoid bar queues. We were hoping for a lower price, but the legal department said that they are required to keep the same prices as in the ship’s bars. To order, please contact the booking department with your booking reference handy. Call Viking Line on 08-4524000 to book the drink package. Or login online to your booking. Merch: Lower prices on T-shirts & hand-fans - cash only! Since we couldn’t offer a lower price for the drink packages, we’ve instead lowered the prices on cruise t-shirts: 200 SEK (instead of 300) and hand-fans (100 SEK) at the C.U.M. Clubwear shop (open 22.00-01.00 at the Backstage club, deck 5). They also sell after-party tickets (200SEK cash only). And finally, here are all set times.
