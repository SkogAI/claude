Dear all,

I’m happy to share with you our summer sales competitions final results.


1.       Jack Daniels pub promotion - in total we increased sales 23% and sold 226L more compared with last year same period. Almost all ships improved their results.  You can find results from attached file. According to all criteria’s Baltic Queen pub team is the winner – they increased sales 38% (second best result), sold Sinatra Select 12 bottles (last year only 1) and their pax increase was also very good. Congratulation to Baltic Queen pub team – please send me number how many crew members you have in your pub who were involved with competition success latest end of next week. Supplier can organize then prizes for you;



2.       Bacardi Breezers sundeck promotion - summer was wonderful and also Breezer sales – all ships increased sales 51% and we sold in total 35 043 Breezers more compared with last year. Thank you for the great job! Almost all ships increased their sales and reached the target (except Silja Serenade). Bestselling flavors are still Lime and Watermelon. Best result did Galaxy and prize goes to Sweden in this time :)

Galaxy - please let me know some suitable dates in November for the prize event (dinner and drinks) latest end of next week. I believe you would like to organize it in Stockholm? 15 crew members can attend;



3.       Segafredo coffee sales competition for cafeterias -  specialty coffees sales increased in Tallink ships 10% and in Silja Ships 29% - very good result! In total we sold 6 827 specialty coffees more compared with last year same period. Regular black coffee sales decreased in Tallink ships 5% and in Silja ships 6% and in total we sold 20 960 cups less. Unfortunately Cold Brew coffee sales dropped a lot – Tallink ships sold 38% less (-1 139 cold brew coffees) and Silja ships sold -13% less (-323 cold brew coffees). It is pity, because summer was perfect for cold drinks.

If we take into account all sales competition criteria’s then from Tallink side Isabelle did the best result and from Silja side Baltic Princess is the winner – congratulations! One person from Isabelle cafeteria and one person from Baltic Princess cafeteria will get trip to Amsterdam Segafredo factory. Trip takes place on week-44 (exact dates and agenda will be sent soon). Isabelle and Princess – please send me your representative name and birth date, nationality and trip departure city information latest end of next week. Then supplier can start arrange flights and accommodation bookings. Also please let me know how many employers you have in Isabelle and Princess cafeteria who were involved with the coffee sales – rest of team members will get goodie bags from Meira.

Thank you for the good job and congratulations to all winners!
