Ollama now supports GitHub's Copilot CLI, the terminal agent that works directly with repositories on GitHub:
 
 * Bring GitHub context into your session: refer to issues and pull requests (e.g. tell me about issue #15291) to bring comments,
   diffs, and statuses into your current session.

 * Delegate to subagents: use /fleet to break a task into subagents working in parallel across your codebase, then finalize
   changes in your favorite editor such as VS Code.

 * Works inside your existing guardrails: the Copilot CLI runs with your existing Business or Enterprise policies to keep your
   repositories and data safe.

 


Get started

Download Ollama [https://ollama.com/download], and then run:
 
ollama launch copilot
 
For more information, see the documentation [https://docs.ollama.com/integrations/copilot-cli].
