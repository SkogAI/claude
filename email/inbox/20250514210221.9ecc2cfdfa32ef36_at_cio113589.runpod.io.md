RTX 5090s are now on RunPod Serverless ( https://www.runpod.io/console/serverless )

****************************************************************************************
5090s on Serverless: Speed Meets Simplicity ( https://www.runpod.io/console/serverless )
****************************************************************************************

You can now run workloads on the RTX 5090 via RunPod Serverless-unlocking one of the world’s fastest GPUs with zero setup, zero overhead, and sub-second cold starts.

The 5090 was already breaking records in our LLM benchmarks, delivering 65,000+ tokens/sec ( https://blog.runpod.io/the-rtx-5090-is-here-serve-65-000-tokens-per-second-on-runpod/ ) on inference workloads. With Serverless, you don’t need to spin up a Pod or manage infrastructure - just send your job and go. That means faster performance and faster time-to-value.

It’s the best of both worlds: top-tier performance, now available as a fully managed, on-demand service.

***************
Why it matters:
***************

*
Serverless delivers the full power of 5090s with zero overhead
* No pod orchestration needed - just deploy and run
* Perfect for time-sensitive tasks, demos, or production endpoints

*******************
How to get started:
*******************

* Choose “Serverless” from the left menu
* Select the image tab
* Pick an image model (they are known to be more performant on 5090s)
* Select 32 GB (this will select the 5090)
Deploy Your AI On Serverless ( https://www.runpod.io/console/serverless )
